Post MSN APRN Certificate Program

- Adult-Gerontology Acute Care NP (AGNP - Acute)
    - Clinical hours: 705
    - Acute care hospital, long-term care facilities, physician offices, urgent care centers, and ambulatory care centers.
    - Objectives: Assessment of health status, clinical decision making, management plan and therapeutic interventions, advocacy for cost-effective and quality care, professional development.

Curriculum:

- Clinical Core Courses
    - Advanced Concepts of Pathophysiology
    - Advanced Health Assessment
    - Clinical Pharmacology for Advanced Nursing Practice
- Adult-Gerontology Nurse Practitioner - Acute Specialty Courses
    - Management of Acutely Ill Adults I
    - Management of Acutely Ill Adults II
    - Management of Acutely Ill Adults Practicum

Admission Information:

- Offered once a year
- Fall and spring courses are 16 weeks, summer courses are 10 weeks
- No campus visits required
- English proficiency requirements for non-native speakers
- Accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education
