Community English Classes at the Intensive English Program

Program Overview:

- The Intensive English Program at the University of Southern Indiana offers its services to community members who are not enrolled at the university.
- All levels of English proficiency are offered in eight-week sessions with two sessions available each semester.
- Placement test is required for class selection.

Eligibility:

- Green card holders
- Spouses of expatriates working in the U.S.
- International students currently taking full-time classes at USI, but seeking additional language enrichment opportunities
- Other cases will be considered on an individual basis

Payment:

- Fees are determined based on the individual's status in the community.
- An invoice will be issued to the enrollee, to be paid within the first week of classes.
- Payment can be made in cash, check, money order, or credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express) at the Bursar's office.

Add/Drop Policy:

- Community members must read and sign the Add/Drop Policy form established for the community classes.
- If a community member drops a class after the first week of the session, they are responsible for paying the full amount.

For more information, contact Terry McIntosh or Dr. Emilija Zlatkovska, Director of the Intensive English Program.
