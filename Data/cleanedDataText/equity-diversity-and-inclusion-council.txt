Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion Council (EDI Council)

Mission and Vision:

The Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion Council at the University of Southern Indiana (USI) is dedicated to promoting a diverse and inclusive campus environment. Its mission is to enhance understanding, celebrate differences, and foster a sense of belonging for all members of the USI community. The council strives to create a campus culture that values equity, inclusion, and respect.

Diversity Resources:

USI recognizes that its strength lies in the diversity of its members. The university is committed to continuously improving its environment and celebrating human differences. The Diversity Resources webpage provides information and support for students, faculty, administrators, staff, and alumni.

Student Organizations:

USI offers a variety of student organizations that promote diversity and inclusivity. These organizations provide opportunities for students to engage with diverse perspectives, cultures, and identities.

Minutes and Agendas:

The EDI Council publishes minutes and agendas from its meetings to keep the campus community informed about the progress and discussions regarding equity, diversity, and inclusion initiatives.

Campus Inclusion Committees & Councils:

USI has established various committees and councils focused on inclusion and diversity. These groups work to ensure that the campus remains inclusive and diverse and develop strategies to promote equity and fairness.

Campus Events:

USI organizes campus events that support and promote diversity, equity, and inclusion. These events provide opportunities for education, dialogue, and celebration of diverse cultures, backgrounds, and experiences.
