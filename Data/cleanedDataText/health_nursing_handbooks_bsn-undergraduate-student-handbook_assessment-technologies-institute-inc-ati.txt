ATI (Assessment Technologies Institute, Inc.):

- Comprehensive program to identify student mastery of content in the undergraduate nursing program and prepare for the NCLEX exam.
- ATI exams are administered for most nursing courses.
- ATI content exams count towards the course grade.
- Students must achieve Level 2 proficiency on the first attempt to receive full ATI points.
- Remediation materials and retake opportunities are provided for students who don't achieve Level 2 proficiency.
