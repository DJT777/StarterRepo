University of Southern Indiana (USI) Interfraternity Council (IFC) Recruitment

IFC Recruitment Process:

- Fall Semester: The IFC participates in a formal recruitment process at the beginning of the semester.
- Planned Events: The IFC organizes events for Potential New Members to meet fraternity men.
- IFC Sponsored Events: These events are mandatory for all men interested in joining a fraternity.
- Fraternity Events: After the IFC events, individual fraternities host their own events that interested men can attend.
- Formal Bid Day: The recruitment process concludes with a formal bid day.
- Ongoing Bids: Fraternities may continue to give out bids throughout the semester and the rest of the academic year.

Participating Fraternities:

- Kappa Alpha Order
- Lambda Chi Alpha
- Sigma Pi
- Sigma Tau Gamma
- Tau Kappa Epsilon

Recruitment Eligibility:

- Degree-seeking student at the University of Southern Indiana with a minimum of 12 credit hours.
- Minimum GPA of 2.6 based on the most recent educational experience (high school or college).

Registration:

- Interested individuals must register for recruitment to be eligible to receive a bid.
