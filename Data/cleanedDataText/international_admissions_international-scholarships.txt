International Scholarships at University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Overview:

    A limited number of international scholarships are offered on a competitive basis to well-qualified degree-seeking students.
    Interested students need to complete a separate scholarship application in addition to the application for admission.
    Scholarships are primarily for incoming freshmen, but transfer students may also be considered.

Global Leader Scholarships:

    Equivalent to in-state tuition waivers, estimated at approximately $10,656 per academic year.
    Easy application process: Complete one application to apply for all international student scholarship opportunities.

Eligibility Requirements:

    Must first apply for admission and be accepted to USI before completing the International Student Scholarship Application.
    Minimum cumulative high school GPA of 3.25 or higher on a 4.0 scale.
    Must have or plan to have a non-immigrant visa (F-1 or J-1 student visa).
    Preference given to freshmen (students who have not attended any colleges or universities after high school, excluding ESL study).
    Applicants can start in USI's Intensive English Program first, but must complete the International Student Scholarship application with original application/acceptance to USI.
    Priority consideration deadlines: March 15th for the Fall term, October 1st for the Spring term.

Application Components:

    Application form.
    Two recommendation forms with recommendation letters.
    Two essays.
    Scholarship review committee considers various factors to award available scholarships to appropriate applicants.

#YouAreWelcomeHere Scholarship:

    USI is a proud participant in the #YouAreWelcomeHere scholarship program.
    Grants in-state tuition waivers to two selected scholars.
    Visit the YAWH website (https://www.youarewelcomehereusa.org/2021-scholarship) for application details.
