Additional Information:

- Consult with CAPS, Dean of Students Office, or Housing and Residence Life for assistance in addressing specific situations concerning students' mental health and well-being.
- Encourage students to schedule appointments with counselors at CAPS for support with stressors and challenges they may be facing.
- The USI CARE Team provides confidential and proactive support and resources for students in need. Referrals can be made to the CARE Team through their website.
- Individual one-on-one assistance is available by contacting CAPS during office hours.
