Steps to Study Abroad

Before You Apply:

Considerations for choosing a study abroad program.
Attend a study abroad information session to gather information about courses offered, timeframe, finances, and interests.
Research funding options, including scholarships and other sources of financial assistance.

Review the USI Study Abroad Travel Policy to understand travel restrictions based on government advisories.

You've Chosen a Program. Now What?

Make an appointment with a Study Abroad Advisor to discuss next steps.
Consult with your Academic Advisor to understand course offerings and plan your academic schedule.
Contact Student Financial Assistance to determine how your current financial aid can be applied to program costs.
Meet with the Student Financial Success Center to establish a financial plan for an affordable program.
Discuss your plans with parents or other important individuals in your life.

Additional Resources:

Watch the study abroad information session to learn more about studying abroad.
