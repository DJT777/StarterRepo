Teacher Education Department

    Accreditation:
        - The Educator Preparation Provider (EPP) at the University of Southern Indiana is accredited by the Council for the Accreditation of Educator Preparation.
        - The Teacher Education Department is the hub of the EPP, overseen by the Dean of the Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education.

Reports and Dashboards:

    CAEP Dashboard: Online tool sharing data on USI's progress in increasing the quantity and quality of educators.
    Title II Reports: Reports reflecting the quality of the teacher preparation program.
    Educator Preparation Program Report: Annual report required by the Indiana Department of Education, including licensure test data, candidate GPA data, attrition/retention/completion data, principal/employer satisfaction data, teacher/alumni satisfaction data, and teacher effectiveness data.
