Advising Information:

- Students are advised by full-time mathematics faculty throughout their time at the university.
- First-year students, transfer students preparing for admission to their major, and Pott College students seeking academic and career advising can seek help from the Student Advising and Resource Center.
- There is a faculty list available for students who bypass the Advising Center or have already settled into their major coursework.
