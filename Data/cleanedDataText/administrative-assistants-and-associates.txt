Administrative Assistants and Associates (AA&A)

    Profile:
        Longest-running employee organization on the USI campus, established in 1969.
        Aims to promote unity among USI employees, foster professional development, and provide financial support to eligible USI students.

    Membership:
        Open to all USI employees, including faculty, support staff, and administrative staff.

    Activities:
        Monthly activities held between September and April.
        Activities may include off-campus trips and guest speakers who provide entertainment and offer professional and personal development advice.

    Benefits:
        Networking opportunities with employees across the USI campus.
        Professional development.
        Support for eligible USI students through a scholarship fund.

    Contact Information:
        Email: Provided on the site.
        Location: 8600 University Blvd, Evansville, IN 47712.

    Social Media Channels: Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram.
