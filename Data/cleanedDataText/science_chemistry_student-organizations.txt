Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education - Student Organizations

American Chemical Society (ACS) - Student Chapter:

- A professional organization for students interested in chemistry as a career.
- Attend national chemistry meetings and undergraduate research conferences.
- Host guest lectures for Chemistry seminars and tour chemical industries.
- Participate in service activities on campus and in the community.
- Faculty advisor: Dr. Ken Walsh.

TIMMY Global Health:

- A community-based organization that expands access to healthcare globally.
- Work on local activities and international projects through trips, fundraising, and volunteer service.
- Advisor: Dr. Kim Delaney.

Pre-Professional Health Club (PPHC):

- For students interested in pursuing health-focused professional careers (Medicine, Dentistry, Optometry, etc.).
- Offers resources and networking opportunities for students.
- Advisor: Dr. Alex Champagne.

Be The Match:

- Connects patients with their donor match for bone marrow transplants.
- Work on awareness, bone marrow registry drives, and fundraisers.
- Advisor: Dr. Becky Sparks-Thissen.

Dental Club:

- For students pursuing careers in dentistry.
- Provides opportunities to learn from experts, network with students, and serve the community.
- Activities include inviting speakers, teaching oral hygiene in elementary schools, and volunteering.
- Advisor: Dr. Alex Champagne.
