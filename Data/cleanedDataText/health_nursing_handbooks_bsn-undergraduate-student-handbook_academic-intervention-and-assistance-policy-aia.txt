        Academic Intervention and Assistance Policy (AIA):
            Provides assistance to beginning nursing students experiencing academic difficulties.
            Students earning less than 75% on exams are required to meet with the Course Coordinator or faculty designee to develop a plan for achieving mastery of nursing content.
            Referral resources for test-taking strategies and study groups are available.
