Student Occupational Therapy Association (SOTA)

- Purpose: Facilitate further education, foster communication, and involve community occupational therapy practitioners.
- Membership: Open to occupational therapy students, occupational therapy assistant students, and anyone interested in the occupational therapy profession.
- Meeting Frequency: Monthly during Fall and Spring semesters in the Health Professions Building.
- Membership Dues: $15, used for sending delegates to the National American Occupational Therapy Association (AOTA) conference to represent USI's Occupational Therapy and Occupational Therapy Assistant programs.
