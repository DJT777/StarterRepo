Small World Program

    Description:
        The Small World Program is a cultural exchange program for American and international students at the University of Southern Indiana (USI).
        Monthly activities are organized throughout the academic year.
        The program aims to foster engagement and friendship between American and international students in a fun and casual setting.
        Benefits:
            American students can learn about different cultures and make friends with international students.
            International students can improve their English language skills and gain insight into American culture.
