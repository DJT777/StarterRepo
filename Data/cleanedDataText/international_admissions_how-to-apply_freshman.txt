Freshman International Admissions at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Application Process:

1. Eligibility: You should apply as a freshman if you have earned a diploma from an accredited high school but have never attended a college or university before (except for ESL).
2. Online Application: Complete the online Admissions Application and pay the $40 application fee.
3. Official Academic Records: Submit Official Academic Records from all secondary schools you have attended. Submit your records online.
   - Note for Chinese School Applicants: Do not submit your records through the upload form. Arrange for a verification report of your school transcript and diploma to be sent directly to USI Admissions by the CDGDC or CSSD.
4. Official Proof of English Proficiency: Submit official proof of English Proficiency.
5. ACT or SAT Scores: Submit either official ACT or SAT scores, if required.
6. Explanation of Gap in Education: If you have taken time off from school, provide an explanation of what you have been doing since graduating from high school until your planned enrollment at USI.
7. Admission Decision: Once all requirements are received and reviewed, an admission decision will be made. Students are notified of their admission decision by email.
8. NACES Certified Credentials Evaluation: USI reserves the right to require a third-party NACES certified credentials evaluation for complex educational backgrounds.

I-20/DS-2019 Documents:

After completing the application process to USI, submit your required immigration documents to receive your I-20 or DS-2019.

Application Options:

Choose the appropriate application from the following options:
- Intensive English Program - Fall 2023
- Intensive English Program - 2024
- Undergraduate Admission - 2024 USI Application
- Undergraduate Admission - 2024 Common App
