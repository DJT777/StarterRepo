Alumni Spotlight - Kyle Kamp

- Profile:
  - USI Degree: Bachelor of Science in Food and Nutrition - Dietetics in 2013
  - From: Evansville, Indiana
  - Currently lives in: Boise, Idaho
- Current Occupation: Owner of Valley to Peak Nutrition, a nutrition consultation business with a focus on helping people prepare for and perform optimally in the mountains.
- Career Background:
  - Clinical dietitian in a hospital in Boise, Idaho, covering general surgery, cardiovascular ICU/Cardio-thoracic ICU, and Telemetry.
  - Coordinated an outpatient nutrition program at the same hospital.
- USI Experience:
  - Chose USI initially due to proximity to hometown and felt comfortable staying due to the professors' caring nature and dedication to student success.
- Preparation for Career:
  - USI's dietetics program taught the importance of self-direction and organization, skills vital to owning and operating a business.
  - Emphasis on preparing for the required dietetic internship and achieving necessary qualifications.
- Involvement:
  - Member of the Food and Nutrition Student Organization (FNSO) throughout the program.
- Favorite Memory: Bonding with the nine ladies in the graduating dietetics class and supporting each other throughout the program.
- Recommendation: USI offers a great campus environment with a university feel, small class sizes, and dedicated professors. The Food and Nutrition/dietetics program equips students with a strong knowledge base, comparable to larger universities.
