    Welcome Message:

    The Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education at USI is dedicated to empowering the next generation of inventive thinkers and transforming lives through the power of discovery. The faculty, staff, and alumni are committed to academic excellence and provide hands-on learning experiences to each student. The college fosters a culture of caring and connection, and strives to prepare students to be creative problem solvers and influential leaders.
