Food and Nutrition Facilities at the College of Nursing and Health Professions

    Location: Health Professions Center at the College of Nursing and Health Professions.

    Facilities:
        Quantity Foods Kitchen: Equipped with modern food preparation equipment and a cooking demonstration station.
        Assessment Lab: Used for completing measurements such as anthropometrics (body measurements), body composition, blood pressure, glucose, and cholesterol.
        Counseling and Education Room: Contains up-to-date nutrition education materials, models, curriculum, journals, and audiovisuals. Used for client consultations and telehealth sessions.
        Learning Resource Center: Interdisciplinary space with computers and software for recipe analysis, diet analysis, and data work.
    Virtual Tour: A 360-degree tour of Food and Nutrition's classroom facilities is available.
