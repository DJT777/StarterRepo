Core 39:

Students pursuing undergraduate degrees in the College of Nursing and Health Professions must also complete the University Core 39. The core provides the foundation for all baccalaureate degrees at the University of Southern Indiana and helps students acquire discipline-specific competence and broader knowledge and skills. View the classes included in Core 39 or consult with an advisor for more information.
