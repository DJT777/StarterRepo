- News and Awards:
    - USI students won first place in the Indiana Society of Radiologic Technologists Quiz Bowl (October 25, 2019)
    - Logan Kluesner of USI came in first place, bringing the traveling trophy back to USI for a second year in a row
    - USI had five other students compete in the individual, single elimination competition
    - Megan Webster, Carly Price, Samantha Shelton, Autumn Rickard, and Jordan Fox were among the competitors
    - Two faculty members from the USI Radiologic and Imaging Sciences programs were elected to the ISRT board of directors
    - Jeremy Smith elected as vice president and Katherine Peak elected as treasurer
    - Heather Schmuck and Samantha Sawyer retired from the ISRT board
