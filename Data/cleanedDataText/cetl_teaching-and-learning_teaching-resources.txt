During the COVID-19 pandemic, the Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning (CETL) acknowledges the challenges faced by faculty as they adapt their teaching methods. Faculty members can find support and expertise from various resources across USI, including colleagues, staff in Online Learning, Information Technology, Rice Library, CETL, and Disability Resources.
