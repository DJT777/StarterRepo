College of Nursing and Health Professions - Respiratory Therapy Program

Admission Information:

Program Capacity: Accepts 16 students
Number of Applications Received: 30+
Class Selection: Once a year, with varying deadlines
Admission Requirements: Applicants are scored based on GPA and prior or current healthcare experience
Program Acceptance Acknowledgment Deadline: Midnight on the specified deadline date
Required GPA: Minimum grade point average of 2.5 or above in prerequisite courses
Prerequisites: Grades of at least "C" in specific courses
Alternate Position: No guarantee of acceptance, applicants need to reapply
Incomplete Prerequisites: conditional acceptance is possible if completed by the program start
Program Duration: 5-semester program (120 credit hours total)
Degree Awarded: Bachelor of Science Degree in Respiratory Therapy
Official Transcripts: High school and college transcripts required for USI Admissions office and program separately
Application Process: Apply to USI first, then apply to the program
Tuition: Information available on the Financial Aid Cost of Attendance page
Acceptance Notification: Varies, depends on the application deadline each year
Accreditation: Program accredited by the Commission on Accreditation for Respiratory Care (CoARC)
Key Program Features:
    All clinical hours can be completed locally.
