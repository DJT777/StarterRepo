Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning

    Syllabus Template:

        Provides a guide for developing an effective and learner-centered syllabus for USI courses.
        Includes required elements and policy statements for the course syllabus.
        Updates have been made, including recommended safety procedures, links, and information from the Institutional Equity Office.
        Template also includes comments with guidance and links to additional resources.

    Resources for Developing the Syllabus:

        Developing Course Objectives and Student Learning Outcomes:
            Provides guidance and examples for writing course objectives and learning outcomes.
        
        Designing an Effective Syllabus:
            Offers strategies and resources for revising syllabi to improve the student experience.
        
        Tips for Creating an Engaging Syllabus:
            Provides design elements and suggestions for making the syllabus more relevant and student-centered.
        
        Considering Accessibility and Inclusion:
            Offers tips and resources for making the syllabus accessible and inclusive for all students.
        
        Course Workload Estimator:
            A tool that estimates a student's out-of-class workload based on planned reading, assignments, and assessments.
