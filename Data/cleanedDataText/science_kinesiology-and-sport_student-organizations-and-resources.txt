Student Organizations - Exercise Science, Kinesiology, and Sport Club

    Purpose: Provide information and experiences relevant to future careers in an Exercise Science, Kinesiology, or Sport Management field.
    Activities: Monthly educational meetings, volunteer opportunities, social and recreational activities.

    Directions to the Human Performance Lab:
        Enter 8600 University Blvd Evansville, IN 47712 into your GPS to reach the campus.
        The Human Performance Lab is located in the Screaming Eagles Center (SEC), the first building on the left after the main parking lot.
        Park in the last bay of the main parking lot (Lot C).
        Enter the building through the front door facing the main parking lot and go up to the second floor.
        Look for a big Eagle statue.
        The room is SEC266.
