Accounts Payable at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Payment Methods:

Direct Pay Instructions:
- Contact: Orr Center, OC 0029
- Phone: 812-464-1967
- Email: Provided on the site
- Follow the instructions in the Direct Pay Form:
    - Enter payee information
    - Provide address for payment mailing or direct deposit
    - Describe the transaction and business purpose
    - Answer additional information questions
    - Enter Fund, Orgn, Acct, and optional Actv numbers
    - Allocate amount to each Fund/Orgn/Acct/Actv combination
    - Obtain financial manager's signature (can be electronic or physical)
    - Save supporting documentation electronically
    - Submit the completed form and supporting documentation via email to accounts.payable@usi.edu

Frequently Misunderstood Points:
- A copy of the direct pay form is not mailed with the check.
- Description in the direct pay form should be in layman's terms, not copied exactly from supporting documentation.
- The Fund, Orgn, and Acct numbers each consist of five digits for a total of 15 digits.
- Only authorized financial managers can approve direct pay forms.
- Accounts Payable does not call or fax orders to vendors.
- Departments should make their own photocopies of forms.
