Introduction:
- The College of Nursing and Health Professions (CNHP) Advising Center welcomes students to the CNHP family at USI.
- The advisors are here to assist students in navigating college life and provide guidance.
- Advisors can help with academic planning and support, but students are responsible for keeping up with their emails and scheduling appointments.

Finding Your Advisor:
- Access DegreeWorks through your myUSI account to see your advisor's name.
- Look for the graduation cap icon, click on it, and your advisor's name will be listed in the information box.
- Click on your advisor's name to email them directly.

Advisor Assignments:
- Incoming freshmen and "pre" major students are assigned professional academic advisors within the Academic Advising Center.
- Once accepted into a program, students will transition to faculty advisors.

Appointment Scheduling:
- Use AdvisorTrac, available through myUSI EagleApps, to schedule appointments with your academic advisor.
- Graduate students and degree completion students should contact the Program Chair of their program for assistance.

News:
- Spring and Summer 2023 registration opens on October 24. Make sure to meet with your advisor before registering.
- Access AdvisorTrac through myUSI to schedule appointments with your advisor.
- Stay connected with USI through social media channels, including Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.
