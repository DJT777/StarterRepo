University of Southern Indiana (USI) College of Nursing and Health Professions

Health Services Program

Mission: "Promoting innovative and work-force aligned healthcare education through engaged interprofessional learning, research, and strategic partnerships".

Degree Options:

1. Bachelor of Science in Health Services (Generalist Option)
2. Bachelor of Science in Health Services with specialization in Gerontology
3. Bachelor of Science in Health Services with specialization in Health Promotion and Worksite Wellness
4. Bachelor of Science in Health Services with specialization in Public Health
5. Bachelor of Science in Health Services with degree completion option for those with active clinical licenses

Career Opportunities:
- Various careers in the health services industry
- Increased job market competitiveness with a degree from USI
- Preparation for graduate school placements

Unique Opportunities:
- Advanced subject matter coverage
- Experienced faculty with healthcare industry knowledge
- Small class sizes for personalized instruction
- Professional internships for workplace experience
- Research projects with faculty researchers
- Development of critical thinking, communication, and writing skills

Faculty:
- Full-time faculty members with expertise in health services

Minor Options:
- Minor in Health Promotion and Worksite Wellness
- Minor in Interdisciplinary Gerontology
- Minor in Public Health
