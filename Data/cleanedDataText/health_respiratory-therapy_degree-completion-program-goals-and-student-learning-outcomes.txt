RRT to BS in Respiratory Therapy Online Program

Program Overview:
- 100% online degree completion program for respiratory therapists in Indiana.
- Accredited by the Commission on Accreditation for Respiratory Care (CoARC).
- Allows respiratory therapist to advance their skills and career, and gain new expertise with a BSRT degree.

Admission Requirements:
- Completion of an associate degree in a CoARC accredited respiratory therapy program.
- Cumulative grade point average of 2.3 on a 4.0 scale.
- RRT credential through the National Board for Respiratory Care (NBRC).
- Submission of official transcripts and a $40 application fee.
- Non-native English speakers must provide official TOEFL or IELTS scores.

Program and Graduation Requirements:
- Transfer credits from an accredited community college or university are accepted.
- No limitation on the number of credits that may be transferred.
- Minimum of 30 credit hours to be earned from USI.
- Core respiratory therapy credits (22-30 credits) and non-respiratory support courses (not more than 19 credits).
- Total program requirements: 120 credits (39 credits at the 300/400 level).

Program Highlights:
- Offered entirely online with seven-week courses and multiple start dates.
- Opportunities for credit validation based on board certification.
- Flexible class schedules for working respiratory therapists.
- Cost-effective and accessible format.

Career Opportunities:
- Prepare for advanced clinical respiratory therapist jobs.
- Acquire skills in critical thinking, problem-solving, time management, and communication.
- Advance in your career, acquire advanced respiratory therapist jobs, or continue education in other healthcare specialties.

Program Accreditation:
- Accredited by the Commission on Accreditation for Respiratory Care (CoARC).

Application Deadlines:
- Six application deadlines per year.
- Applications can be submitted for spring, summer, or fall semesters.

Tuition and Financial Assistance:
- Tuition is $345 per credit hour.
- Student financial assistance is available, including the Adult Student Grant for Indiana residents.

Program Delivery and Technology Requirements:
- Online courses delivered through a learning management system.
- Reliable computer and internet access required.

Get Started:
- Apply online and submit necessary documents.
- Complete the program in as few as 4 semesters.

Connect with USI:
- Social media channels: Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram.
- Contact information provided on the website.
