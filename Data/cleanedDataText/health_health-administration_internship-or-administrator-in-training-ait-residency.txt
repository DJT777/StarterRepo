Health Administration Internship or Administrator-in-Training (AIT) Residency

- Description: Offers students in the Bachelor of Science in Health Administration program the opportunity to apply learned theories and skill sets in the healthcare field. Completed under the supervision of a qualified healthcare professional in a community setting.
- AIT Residency: Consists of 1040 hours of residency in a long-term care facility, preparing students for eligibility for Health Facility Administrator licensure in Indiana.
- Internship and AIT conducted according to Association of University Programs in Health Administration (AUPHA) guidelines.

Pre-requisites for Internship and AIT:

- Cumulative GPA of 2.75 prior to the start of HA 481 or GERO 499
- Successful completion of HP 475: Professionalism in Healthcare with a grade of C or better
- Successful completion of GERO 425: Health Facilities Administration with a grade of C or better (AIT only)
- Completion of CastleBranch Account and requirements (Tuberculosis Module, OSHA Module, HIPPA Module)

Internship and AIT Handbooks available for more information.
