Requirements for Admission to Diagnostic Medical Sonography Program:

    Acceptance to the University of Southern Indiana.
    Declaration of major in Pre-Diagnostic Medical Sonography at the Registrar's Office.
    Completion of prerequisite courses with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0.
    Submission of an online application to the Diagnostic Medical Sonography program after August 1.
    Submission of a signed and dated narrative addressing specific questions.
    Verification of observation in general, vascular, and cardiac areas of sonography (optional for Fall 2023 application period due to COVID-19).
    Apply by submitting all required materials no later than September 15 of the desired admission year.

How to Apply:

    Ensure that all admission requirements are met.
    Fill out the ONLINE DMS application.
    Submit all necessary materials by the specified deadline.
