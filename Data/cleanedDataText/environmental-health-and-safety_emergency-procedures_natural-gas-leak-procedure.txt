Natural Gas Leak Procedure

Contact Information: 
- Phone: 812-464-8600
- Email: [email protected]

Procedure:
- All gas leaks or suspicious odors should be reported immediately.
- Contact Security at 812-464-1845 to report the gas leak or suspected leak.
- Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) will perform monitoring and evaluation procedures to locate the source of the gas odor.
- If the source cannot be found, EHS will contact the local gas company for an on-site evaluation.
- If evacuation is required due to the gas leak, follow the university's evacuation procedures.
- For any questions or further assistance, contact EHS at 812-461-5393 during regular hours, or Security at 812-464-1845 after hours.
