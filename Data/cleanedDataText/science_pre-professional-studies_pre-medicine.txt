University of Southern Indiana (USI) - Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education Pre-Medicine Program

Overview:
The Pre-Medicine program at USI offers unique opportunities and personalized advising for students interested in pursuing a medical career. The program provides support in choosing relevant courses, extracurricular projects, and pre-professional activities.

Unique Opportunities:
- Pre-Medical Sciences Certificate: Offers a directed curriculum to help organize Pre-Medical training. Available for undergraduates, post-baccalaureates, and non-degree seeking students.
- B/MD Program: A dual degree program in collaboration with the Indiana University School of Medicine. Accepted applicants receive full undergraduate tuition at USI and a provisional seat at the IU School of Medicine. Additional prerequisites may be required.

Outstanding Success:
- USI pre-med students score well above the national average on the MCAT.
- Access to extensive laboratory facilities and hands-on experience with advanced technology.
- Pre-med courses taught by dedicated teaching faculty.
- Support in preparing for the application process, including practice MCATs, mock interviews, and guidance.

Information and Resources:
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) page with information about the path to Medical School, applications, and the MCAT.
- 4 Year Plans available for Biology, Biochemistry, and Chemistry.
