Intensive English Program (IEP) - University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Mission Statement:
The IEP at USI strives to provide quality English language instruction and cultural, social, and academic orientation to non-native students. The program prepares students for success in university academic programs or in achieving professional and personal language goals. The program supports students' integration into the target academic and general culture while respecting their own cultures, traditions, and values. It also enhances the international dimensions of the university and connects international students to the campus and local communities.

Goals for Students:
- Prepare international students for academic studies at USI or use of English language professionally or in social contexts
- Provide ongoing support to international students through various services
- Connect international students with other students at USI and the local community to immerse them in American culture

Goals for Faculty:
- Engage faculty members with high levels of professional conduct and teaching skills in teaching IEP students
- Support and engage faculty members in continuous professional development and program development
- Stay informed and up to date with research supporting best teaching practices

Long Term Goals for the Program:
- Sustain the program's accreditation with the Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA)
- Build and promote the program's name and quality reputation through participation in professional associations and recruitment efforts
- Continue serving as an English language proficiency testing center for the TOEFL test
- Develop special short-term language projects and bring groups from international professional institutions to campus for English language skills development and teacher training
- Enhance community outreach with local companies and institutions
- Serve as an advocate for all international students
- Engage with other member organizations like EnglishUSA and NAFSA for additional support and information updates

Accreditation:
The IEP is accredited by the Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA) for the period April 2023 through April 2033. CEA is recognized by the U.S. Secretary of Education as a nationally recognized accrediting agency for English language programs and institutions in the U.S.
