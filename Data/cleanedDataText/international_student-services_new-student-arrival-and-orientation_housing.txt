University of Southern Indiana (USI) Housing and Residence Life

Housing Options:

Residence Halls:
- Primarily for freshmen
- Suite-style housing with two bedrooms and two bathrooms for four students

Campus Apartments:
- Available for all students (except those with families)
- Fully furnished apartments with a full-sized living room and kitchen

Theme Living Communities (TLCs):
- Dynamic and interactive communities for first-year students
- Students who choose to join TLCs live in close proximity to others who share the same major or interest and attend classes together

Additional Information:

Arrival Essential Packet: Available for order at a cost of $50, includes bed linens, comforter, towels, and a laundry basket.

Learn More About Housing: More detailed information about housing options on the USI website.

Learn More About Living Learning Communities: Detailed information about TLCs and their benefits.
