International Students Outreach Program

    Program Overview:
        Coordinated by the Center for International Programs, the International Outreach Program provides international students with opportunities to share their culture and language with American community groups, school children, and local businesses.

    Application:
        Interested students can fill out the Volunteer Form to serve as ambassadors for their country.

    Volunteer Events:
        Information about volunteer events and how to sign up can be found on the International Students Outreach Program's Facebook page.
