College of Nursing and Health Professions - Dental Assisting Program

    Dental Assisting Program Overview:
        - Pass Rates on the Dental Assisting National Board Examination (DANB):
            - 2023: 94%
            - 2022: 98%
            - 2021: 100%
            - 2020: 92%
            - 2019: 91%
            - 2018: 86%
            - 2017: 100%
            - 2016: 100%
            - 2015: 100%
            - 2014: 78%
            - 2013: 95%
    
    Program Goals:
        1. Prepare graduates to be qualified to sit for and successfully complete the Dental Assisting National Board.
        2. Train competent dental assistants.
        3. Promote dental health within regional communities.
        4. Instill professional integrity and ethics.
        5. Advocate for the dental assisting profession.
        6. Encourage active membership and leadership in professional dental assisting organizations.
        7. Encourage commitment to continued personal learning, growth, and education.
