O'Daniel Leadership Programs

    Philosophy:
        Leadership is the expression of one's values and actions.
        Leaders have a responsibility to act with ethics and integrity.
        Leadership is not defined by a position, but by the actions taken.
        It is a lifelong journey of learning, engaging, and serving others to make a positive impact on the community.

    Programs:
        O'Daniel Leadership Programs challenge and empower USI students to develop their leadership skills and abilities.
        Helps students feel more confident in their ability to lead and make a difference.
        Provides opportunities to build essential leadership skills such as communication, critical thinking, self-confidence, motivation, conflict management, goal setting, teamwork, and self-assessment.

    Student Leader Awards:
        Recognizes outstanding student leaders at USI.
