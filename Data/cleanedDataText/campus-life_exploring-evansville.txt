    Explore Evansville during Welcome Week!
        Each year during Welcome Week, the University of Southern Indiana and the Evansville community come together for an event called Explore Evansville.
        Purpose: To welcome incoming students and provide an opportunity for local businesses and organizations to introduce themselves.
        Activities: Local businesses offer promotional items, discounts, coupons, and helpful information directly to students.
        Food Trucks: Locally owned and operated food trucks set up outside the arena to offer free samples to students.
        Date and Time: August 19th, 2023, from 5-8pm.
        Location: Screaming Eagle Arena.
        Businesses interested in hosting a table can register on the provided link by August 4th, 2023.

    Guide to Exploring Evansville:
