Kristy Denton '12, MHA '15

    Profile:
        USI graduate, wife, mother, long-term care administrator, and adjunct instructor for Romain College of Business.
        Competed as Mrs. Southern Indiana in the Mrs. Indiana for America Pageant in South Bend, Indiana, on March 4.
        Had never been involved in pageants before being crowned Mrs. Southern Indiana.
        Holds a degree in Health Services and a Master of Health Administration (MHA) from USI.
        Worked full-time as a physical therapy assistant while studying at USI.
        Started her career in the long-term care industry after interviewing a long-term care administrator for a class project.
        Currently the administrator at Willowdale Village in Dale, Indiana, with American Senior Communities.
        Appreciates the opportunities and supportive environment provided by USI.
