Volunteer USI

    About Volunteer USI:
        Program aimed at communicating and educating the USI community about the benefits of volunteerism.
        Goal to encourage volunteerism and provide resources to promote it.
        Also tracks the impact of volunteer hours on the University and the community.
        
    Who is Volunteer USI for?
        Students, alumni, employees, retirees, family members, and community members.

    Time Commitment:
        One-time Events
        Short-Term Assignments
        Ongoing Commitments

    Volunteer Opportunities:
        One-time events or projects with groups of volunteers on specific days.
        Short-term assignments like serving on planning committees.
        Ongoing opportunities such as reading to hospice patients regularly.
