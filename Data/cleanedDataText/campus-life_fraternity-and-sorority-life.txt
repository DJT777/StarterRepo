    About Fraternity and Sorority Life:
        Purpose: Designed to help students make the most of their time on campus and develop into responsible, social, and contributing members of society.
        Variety: Each fraternity and sorority on USI's campus has its own character, philanthropies, colors, and histories, catering to diverse student interests.
        Values: Greek-lettered organizations are rooted in founding principles that emphasize scholarship, student involvement, service, and friendship.
        Community: Thirteen nationally-affiliated chapters and two governing councils (Interfraternity Council and Panhellenic Council) at USI.
