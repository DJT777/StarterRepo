Distinguished Nursing Alumni Award

    Lois M. (Stallings) Welden, DNP ’14, MSN ’01, BSN ’94, ASN ’83
    Clinical nurse specialist (CNS) with over 40 years of nursing, research, and leadership experience
    2021 recipient of the University of Southern Indiana Distinguished Nursing Alumni Award
    Recognition for being an outstanding mentor, sharing knowledge and critical thinking skills, and advocating for evidence-based practice
    Career Background:
        Worked as a nurse aide at Clinton County Hospital in Frankfort, Indiana, at the age of 16
        Pursued nursing education while working full-time as a nurse aide at Deaconess Hospital, Evansville
        Graduated from the University of Evansville with an associate degree in nursing
        Employed at Deaconess Hospital in various roles, including assistant head nurse and department director
        Currently working full-time as a clinical nurse specialist in the Deaconess Magnet Program and Employee Education Department
        Adjunct faculty at USI since 2015, teaching online courses

USI Nursing Alumni Society

    Purpose:
        Strengthening bonds between nursing alumni, the community, and the University of Southern Indiana
        Mentoring and networking opportunities for nursing alumni
        Promoting the educational advantages of the USI Nursing Programs
        Serving as a liaison for communication and information dissemination
        Supporting student recruitment through visibility and financial assistance
