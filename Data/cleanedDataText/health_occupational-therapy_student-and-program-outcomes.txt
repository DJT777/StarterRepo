    Occupational Therapy Program Goals:
        Entry-level proficiencies of an occupational therapist.
        Recognition of the role of occupation and related concepts in health and wellness.
        Application of theoretical approaches, frameworks, and practice models.
        Understanding of diverse populations and contexts in occupational performance.
        Active learning and development of appropriate ways of knowing and reasoning.
        Understanding of the impact of pathology on individuals and the ethical standards of the profession.
        Collaboration with other professionals in various settings.
        Embrace of person-occupation-context interactions and development of professional aptitudes.
