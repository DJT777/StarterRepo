USI recognizes the achievements of its students, faculty, and staff through a variety of honors and awards. These awards include academic scholarships, research grants, and recognition for academic excellence. USI also acknowledges outstanding student leadership, community service, and athletic performance. The institution's commitment to excellence is reflected in the honors and awards offered, which aim to recognize outstanding achievement in all areas of university life.
