Career Services and Internships

    SkillSurvey Career Readiness

        Identification of strengths and improvement opportunities through evaluator comments and individual behavioral ratings.
        Assessment of proficiency in the 8 NACE competencies.
        Personalized insight into work-related competencies and comparison with other students nationally.

    What is SkillSurvey?

        Online evaluation system that helps determine the most important skills for employers and compares individual skills to peers.
        Accessible to USI students through courses, internships, student employment, and other groups.
        Contact Career Services to request an evaluation.

    Process:

        Students receive an email with a link to access the evaluation.
        Confirm personal information.
        Enter evaluator information.
        Complete the self-evaluation.
        Receives a personalized Career Readiness Report within two weeks.

    Career Readiness Report:

        Provides self-ratings, evaluator ratings, and a comparison to similar students nationally.
        A tool to gauge progress in desired skill areas.
        Example report available for reference.

    Evaluators:

        Evaluations can include ratings from individuals who have worked with the student.
        Evaluators should provide meaningful feedback about behavior in a work environment.
        Suggestions include supervisors, colleagues, or individuals familiar with the student's professionalism.

    Tips for Making the Most of the Evaluation:

        Request honest, meaningful feedback from evaluators.
        Be honest when filling out the self-evaluation.
        Consider areas for improvement.
        Respect evaluators' privacy and ask for permission before using their names or company names.

    Career Readiness:

        Foundation for displaying core competencies essential for success in the workplace.
        Important for new college graduates entering the workforce.
        Provides a framework for career-related goals and outcomes.
        Helps employers identify skills and develop talent.

    NACE Career Ready Competencies:

        Eight career readiness competencies:
            Career and Self-Development
            Communication
            Critical Thinking
            Equity and Inclusion
            Leadership
            Professionalism
            Teamwork
            Technology
