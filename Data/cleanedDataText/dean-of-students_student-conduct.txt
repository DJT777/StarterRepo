- Student Conduct Process
    - Incident reports received from various departments
    - Review of incident reports to determine follow-up
    - Notice of alleged violation sent to student through USI Eagles email
    - Notice includes meeting/hearing details, policy violations/charges, contact information, and process information
    - Minimum two business days notice given before the conduct meeting/hearing
    - Conduct meetings/hearings between Conduct Officer and student
    - Student Conduct Hearing Board may be used for suspension or expulsion cases
    - Appeals process available
