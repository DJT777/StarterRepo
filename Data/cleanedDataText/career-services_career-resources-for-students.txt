Career Resources for Students

Career Services and Internships offer various resources and services to help students with their career development and job search. Some of the resources available include:

Internships and Co-Ops: Information on internships and co-op programs to gain valuable work experience while still in school.

Career Fairs and Events: Listings and information on career fairs and events on campus.

Career Launch/Symplicity: Information on the Career Launch platform, which allows students to search for jobs and internships, upload resumes, and connect with employers.

Information for Employers: Resources and information for employers who are interested in recruiting USI students.

Information for Faculty/Staff: Resources and information for faculty and staff to support students in their career development.

Career Ready Competencies: Information on the skills and competencies that employers look for in job candidates.

Career Outcomes: Information on career outcomes and success of USI graduates.

Frequently Asked Questions: Answers to common questions about career services and internships at USI.

Additional Career Resources: Sample resumes, CVs, and cover letters, interview preparation tips, job listing websites, on-campus job options, and more.
