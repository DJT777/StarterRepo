Center for Campus Life - LGBTQIA+ Resources

The Center for Campus Life at the University of Southern Indiana (USI) is dedicated to creating an inclusive and diverse environment for all students on campus. They strive to celebrate individual identities and ensure that USI remains a safe space for everyone.

University Resources

Gender-Neutral Restrooms:
- USI supports the Gender-Neutral Restroom Initiative, which aims to provide gender-neutral restrooms throughout the campus. Currently, there is a gender-neutral bathroom at the Recreation Fitness and Wellness Center.

How to Be an Ally:
- Students can join the Sexuality and Gender Alliance student organization to support the LGBTQIA+ community at USI.
- Attending events with friends is another way to show support and be an ally.
- Faculty and staff members can help at events and add themselves to the ally list. The ally list provides LGBTQIA+-friendly faculty and staff members on campus.

Additional Resources:
- Tips for Allies
- GLSEN's Pronoun Guide
- LGBTQIA+ Mental Health and Suicide Prevention - American Foundation for Suicide Prevention

Gender Inclusive Housing:
- USI offers gender-inclusive housing options for students living on campus. This option welcomes students of all genders and identities.

Counseling & Psychological Services:
- USI's Counseling and Psychological Services provide free counseling services and resources for students.

For more information or any questions, contact the Center for Campus Life.
