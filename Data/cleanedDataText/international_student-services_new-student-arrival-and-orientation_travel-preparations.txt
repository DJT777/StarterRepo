Traveling to the University of Southern Indiana in Evansville, Indiana, USA

Pre-departure Orientation:

The University of Southern Indiana hosts a pre-departure orientation for newly admitted international students each semester. This live webinar helps students navigate the visa and arrival process. Students have the opportunity to talk with USI's Immigration Advisor, meet Orientation Assistants, and ask questions.

Pre-departure Resources:

- USI Facts: Provides general information about the university.
- Obtaining F-1 or J-1 Visa Information: Information on obtaining student visas.
- Travel Information: Information on traveling to the University, including airport pickup services provided by the Center for International Programs before orientation.
- Arrival in Evansville: Details about arrival requirements, orientation programs, English language courses, housing availability, and transportation arrangements.

Arrival Essential Packet:

Students can order an Arrival Essential Packet ($50 cost), which includes bed linens, a comforter, towels, and a laundry basket.

What to Bring:

An online list of suggestions of what to bring to the University.

Explore Evansville:

Information about Evansville, including a virtual tour and resources for exploring the city and its surroundings.

EducationUSA Pre-Departure Seminars:

Contact local EducationUSA Advising Centers for helpful information and pre-departure webinars.
