Post Master's Certificate in Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP)

College of Nursing and Health Professions

Overview:

- The Family Nurse Practitioner program provides knowledge and experience to provide direct health care services to people of all ages.
- Focuses on health promotion, disease prevention, and management of acute and chronic illnesses.
- Primary setting: ambulatory care, treating patients across various age groups.
- Total of 665 clinical hours.
- Graduates are prepared to synthesize knowledge, make clinical decisions, design care plans, advocate for patients, and demonstrate professional development.

Post Master's Certificate Curriculum:

1. Clinical Core Courses:
   - Advanced Concepts of Pathophysiology (3 credit hours)
   - Advanced Health Assessment (3 credit hours)
   - Clinical Pharmacology for Advanced Nursing Practice (3 credit hours)
   Total: 9 credit hours

2. Family Nurse Practitioner Specialty Courses:
   - Primary Care Nursing of Families I (5 credit hours)
   - Primary Care Nursing of Families II (5 credit hours)
   - Family Nurse Practitioner Practicum (5 credit hours)
   Total: 15 credit hours

Other Information:

- Gainful employment information can be found on the gainful employment website.
- Out-of-state students pursuing distance learning degrees at USI should check their availability, as the programs have limitations for certain states.
- English proficiency requirements apply for non-native English speakers.
- Accreditation: The baccalaureate degree in nursing, master's degree in nursing, Doctor of Nursing Practice, and post-graduate APRN certificate at USI are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education.

Admission Information:

- Program admission is offered once a year.
- Fall and spring courses are 16 weeks long, while summer courses are 10 weeks long.
- No campus visits required.
- Additional information about the University and campus visits can be found on the USI website.

Tuition Information, Nursing & Health Professions Handbook, and contact information also provided on the website.
