International Engagement on Campus

The Center for International Programs at the University of Southern Indiana (USI) offers various programs and initiatives to promote international engagement and enhance the academic experience for students. These programs aim to foster cultural exchange and global awareness among the USI community. Here are some of the programs offered:

1. International Club: The International Club provides a platform for students from different cultures to come together, share their experiences, and celebrate diversity. Through events and activities, the club promotes cultural understanding and friendship among students.

2. Small World Program: The Small World Program connects international students with local families or individuals. The program offers a unique opportunity for cultural exchange and building connections between international students and the local community.

3. USI Friendship Family Program: The Friendship Family Program connects international students with local families who serve as mentors and friends during their stay at USI. The program helps international students navigate their new surroundings and provides a support system.

4. International Students Outreach Program: The International Students Outreach Program aims to engage the local community in supporting international students. It encourages community members to volunteer and get involved in activities that enhance the international students' experience.

5. International Orientation Assistant: International Orientation Assistants are USI students who provide support and assistance to incoming international students during their orientation period. They help with acclimating to campus life, answering questions, and providing guidance.

6. International Education Week: International Education Week is an annual event that celebrates and highlights the importance of international education and exchange. It features various activities, workshops, and cultural events to promote intercultural understanding and global awareness.

For more information about upcoming events and programs, students can visit the Center for International Programs' Facebook and Instagram pages.
