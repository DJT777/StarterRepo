Respiratory Therapy is:

- A career in the healthcare field.
- An exciting and stimulating profession for individuals who enjoy working with people and helping others.
- Involves direct one-on-one patient contact.
- Involves the diagnosis, treatment, management, preventive care, and rehabilitation of patients with cardiopulmonary problems.
- Caring for patients with various respiratory conditions such as asthma, emphysema, pneumonia, respiratory distress, and more.
- Involves the use of medical gases, humidification, aerosols, mechanical ventilation, and other therapeutic techniques.
- Offers a promising future in a fast-paced and expanding medical environment.

RT Occupational Outlook:

- Employment of respiratory therapists is projected to grow by 14% from 2021 to 2031.
- About 9,400 job openings for respiratory therapists are projected each year, on average, over the decade.
- The need for respiratory therapists is expected to stem from the replacement of workers who transfer to different occupations or retire.
