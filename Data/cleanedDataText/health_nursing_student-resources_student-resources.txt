University Division

Mission Statement:
The mission of University Division is to facilitate the academic success of USI students by helping them build a solid foundation for their college careers. The division coordinates various academic programs and services, including academic support services for all USI students and academic advising for first-year students, including those without a declared major. The division comprises of the following:

1. Advising Centers
2. Academic Skills
3. Career Counseling
4. Student Support Services
