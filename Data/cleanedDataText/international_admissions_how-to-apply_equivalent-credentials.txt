Equivalent Credentials

To help determine the specific academic documents required for admission, the University of Southern Indiana (USI) has developed a list of equivalent secondary school credentials. Freshmen must submit complete final secondary school transcripts, and all documents must be official, original, and final. If the documents are not in English, English translations are required. In some cases, a third-party NACES certified credentials evaluation may be requested for complex educational backgrounds.

The list of equivalent secondary school credentials for admission includes:

- Austria: Reifezeugnis/Reifeprufungzeungnis (Maturity Certificate)
- Bahamas: GCE A Level exam results
- Bahrain: Tawjahiya Secondary School Certificate
- Bangladesh: Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC)
- Belgium: Certificate of Higher Secondary Education
- Brazil: Diploma/Certificado de Ensino Medio
- Canada: Completion of senior secondary school—General or Advanced High School Diploma
- China: Upper Middle School Graduation Certificate
- Colombia: Bachiller/Bachillerato
- Costa Rica: Diploma de conclusión de estudios de educación diversificada or Bachillerato
- Egypt: Thanaweya a’ Amma/Shehadet Thanawiya Azharia
- France: Baccalauréat de l’Enseignement de Second Degré/Baccalauréat Général
- Germany: Zeugnis der Allgemeinen Hochschulreife/Abitur
- Haiti: Baccalauréat
- Hong Kong: Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education (HKDSE)
- India: Official grade 12 board exam results
- Indonesia: Surat Tanda Tamat Belajar (STTB) or Ijazah Sekolah Menengah Atas
- International Baccalaureate and British-Style Exams: Official transcript of grades from the International Baccalaureate Organization/General Certificate of Secondary Education (GCSE)
- Iraq: Adadiyah/Certificate of Preparatory Studies
- Israel: Teudat Bagrut
- Italy: Diploma de Maturita, Diploma de Maturita Magistrate, or Diploma di Esame di Stato
- Jamaica: Caribbean Secondary Education Certificate (CSEC) or GCE O Levels
- Japan: Upper Secondary School Leaving Certificate
- Jordan: Tawjihi General Secondary Education Certificate
- Kazakhstan: Attestat of middle (complete) secondary education
- Kenya: Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE)
- Kyrgyzstan: Attestat certificate and table (transcript)
- Latvia: Atestats par Visparejo Videjo Izglitibu
- Malaysia: Sijil Pelajaran/SPM or Sijil Matrikulasi
- Mexico: Certificado de Bachillerato/Título de Bachiller
- Myanmar (Burma): Diploma of Education/Basic Education High School Examination Diploma “A”
- New Zealand: National Certificate in Educational Achievement (NCEA), Level 3
- Nepal: Higher Secondary Certificate
- Nigeria: West African Examinations Council Senior School Certificate
- Pakistan: Higher Secondary Certificate or Intermediate Certificate
- Philippines: Completion of first two years of university study if graduated in grade 10, or completion of 12 years of secondary study
- Qatar: Thanawiya aama Qatari
- Romania: Diploma de bacalaureat
- Russia: Аттестат о среднем (полном) общем образовании/Attest o srednem (polnom) obchshem obrazovanii
- Saudi Arabia: General Secondary Education Certificate
- Singapore: Singapore-Cambridge O Level certificate
- South Africa: National Senior Certificate (NSC)
- South Korea: Senior high school certificate (Inmungye Kodung Hakkyo)
- Spain: Bachillerato-Título de Bachiller
- Switzerland: Certificat de Maturite
- Taiwan: Senior Middle (High) School Diploma
- Thailand: Matayom (Secondary) VI Certificate
- Turkey: Lise Diplomasi/Devlet Lise Diplomasi
- Ukraine: Atestat pro povnu zagal’nu serednyu osvitu
- United Arab Emirates: Thanawiya (Government/Secondary School Leaving Certificate)
- United Kingdom: General Certificate of Secondary Education (GCSE)
- Vietnam: Bang Tot Nghiep Trung Hoc Pho Thong

For more specific information about admission requirements and the application process, interested individuals are advised to contact the Center for International Programs at USI.
