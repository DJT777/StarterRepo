Event Details:

Date: April 8, 2024
Event: Solar Eclipse
Description: Multi-day event featuring speakers, science exploration, education, entertainment, and food.
Location: University of Southern Indiana
Highlights:
- Talk by electrical engineer and retired NASA astronaut Joan Higginbotham as part of the Romain College of Business Innovative Speaker Series.
- Full day of experiential learning and engagement on the day of the eclipse.
- USI campus in the direct path of the eclipse, offering unobstructed sky views and access to educators and exploration.
- Activities for current and prospective students, alumni, and the broader community, including music, food trucks, expert presentations from the Interdisciplinary Colloquium, and hands-on learning opportunities.
- High school students and their families can learn more about academic opportunities at USI and tour the campus and housing during the event.
- Events are free and open to the public.
