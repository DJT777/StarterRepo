Counseling and Psychological Services

Resources for the Community

    Crisis Information: Information on how to access emergency or crisis support.

    Book an Appointment: Information on how to schedule an appointment with Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS).

    Accessing CAPS Services: Details on how to access services provided by CAPS.

    Other Resources: Additional resources available for the community.

    Meet Our Team: Information about the team members of Counseling and Psychological Services.

    Screagles Mental Health Safety Kit: A training video that provides information on ways to help students with their mental health.

    USI CARE TEAM: The University's CARE Team is a cross-functional assessment group chaired by the Dean of Students. It provides confidential, respectful, and proactive support and resources. Anyone can make a referral to the CARE Team to report a student in need or express concern about a student's behavior.
