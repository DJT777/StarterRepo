University of Southern Indiana (USI) Career Services and Internships

Overview:
Career Services and Internships at USI provides comprehensive career development services and support to students and alumni in their transition from college to career. They offer various resources, workshops, events, and assistance with internship and job searches, resume writing, mock interviews, and more.

Contact Information:
- Orr Center, OC 074
- Phone: 812-464-1865
- Email: career@usi.edu

Career Coordinators:
USI has four career coordinators, each assigned to a specific college and group of majors. They can provide guidance and support tailored to the needs of students in those areas.

Principles of Professional Conduct:
The staff at USI Career Services and Internships follow the National Association for Colleges and Employers' Principles for Career Service Professionals. These principles ensure ethical practices in recruitment activities for colleges, universities, and employers.

Location:
Career Services and Internships is located on the lower level of the Orr Center, specifically in room 074.

Connect with USI:
USI's Career Services and Internships can be found on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.
