College of Nursing and Health Professions - Alumni Spotlight: Sylvia Crowder

Profile:
- USI Degree: Bachelor of Science in Food and Nutrition - Dietetics in 2014
- Current Occupation: Assistant Member at Moffitt Cancer Center in Tampa, Florida
- Research focus: Relationships among dietary patterns, eating behaviors, nutritional status, and patient-centered survivorship outcomes in cancer survivors.

Experience at USI:
- Chose USI for the strong emphasis on Health Professions, smaller class sizes, and excellent teachers.
- The College of Nursing and Health Professions prepared Sylvia for her career through the guidance and knowledge of the instructors in the dietetics department.
- Sylvia had the opportunity to work as a diet technician at Deaconess Hospital during her senior year, applying her knowledge from medical nutrition therapy courses.
- Involvement in the Food and Nutrition Student Organization.

Favorite Memory at USI:
- Walking the nature trails on campus with her dog.

Recommendation for Prospective Students:
- Highly recommends USI for its knowledgeable professors and advisors.
- Emphasizes the importance of critical and applied thinking in the dietetics profession.
- Challenges in coursework are balanced by faculty who are passionate about their careers and invested in student success.
