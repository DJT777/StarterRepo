Center for International Programs - Study Abroad

Why Study Abroad:

    USI offers over 100 opportunities to study in another country for a summer, a semester, or a year.
    Study abroad helps students explore the world and discover themselves.

    Find a Study Abroad Program.

Financing Study Abroad:

    USI is committed to making studying abroad affordable and accessible.
    Costs of some programs are based on tuition, room, and board at USI.
    Participants can continue to apply scholarships and financial aid towards program fees.
    Additional study abroad scholarships are available through the Center for International Programs.
