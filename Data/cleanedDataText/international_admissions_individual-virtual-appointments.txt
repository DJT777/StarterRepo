Center for International Programs

- Individual Virtual Appointments
    - Schedule a 30-minute virtual appointment with an international admissions counselor to learn more about USI, the admission and immigration process, and next steps.
    - Appointments available in multiple languages.
    - Does not include academic advising or course registration.
    - Instructions for joining the appointment:
        - On mobile device or tablet: Download the ZOOM Cloud Meetings app, sign up, and click on the appointment link sent via email.
        - On computer: Install Zoom, sign up, and click on the appointment link sent via email.
        - Please schedule the appointment at least 24 hours in advance.
