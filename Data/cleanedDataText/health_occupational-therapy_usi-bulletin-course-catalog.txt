University of Southern Indiana 2023-2024 Undergraduate Bulletin

Welcome!

The Bulletin serves as the official source of information for the University of Southern Indiana's academic programs, courses, policies, and procedures. It should be used as a guide for planning a course of study and meeting graduation requirements.

Navigation:

- Left-hand menu: Use the links to view different sections of the bulletin.
- Search Box: Located in the top left corner, use it to search for specific keywords or phrases.
- Drop-down menu: Found in the top right corner, select different publications like the current Graduate Bulletin or Class Schedule.
- Archived Publications: Link on the left-hand menu to access previous editions of the Bulletin or Class Schedule.

Icon Guide:

- Degree Planner: Provides a print-friendly checklist-style version of degree requirements.
- Add to Portfolio: Allows users to create a personal portfolio using the My Portfolio link.
- Social Media: Share bulletin information on Facebook or Twitter.
- Print-Friendly Format: Generates a pop-up page formatted for neat printing.
- Help: Offers more information about navigating the bulletin.

Contact Information:

The bulletin aims to answer most questions about the University of Southern Indiana. If further information is needed, inquiries should be directed to the appropriate faculty member or University officers. Current information can be found on the University's website, and specific departments can be contacted for more details.

Important Notes:

- Bulletin content, programs, policies, procedures, regulations, fees, and announcements are subject to change. The University reserves the right to add, amend, or repeal any information in the bulletin.
- Student Rights and Responsibilities, as outlined in the Code of Student Behavior, may be subject to change. Students will be notified of any updates via email.
- The University of Southern Indiana is committed to complying with non-discrimination and equal opportunity laws and regulations.
- The campus is tobacco-free.
