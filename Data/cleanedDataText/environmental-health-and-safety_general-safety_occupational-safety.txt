Environmental Health and Safety

Occupational Safety Programs:

1. Blood Borne Pathogens: Training and vaccination offered for employees with potential exposure to blood or other infectious materials.
2. Confined Space Entry: Program developed to protect employees required to enter confined spaces.
3. Lockout / Tag Out: Written program ensuring safe procedures when servicing or maintaining equipment.
4. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Program providing protective equipment for workers.
5. Respiratory Protection: Plan to protect workers from harmful chemical exposures.
6. Industrial Lift Trucks (Forklifts): Program ensuring safe operation of material handling vehicles.
7. Hand and Power Tools: Safety program to prevent injuries when using hand and power tools.
8. Aerial Lift Machines: Program ensuring proper use and training for vehicle-mounted work platforms.
9. Ergonomics: Program to reduce or eliminate hazards related to musculoskeletal disorders.
   - Includes surveys, worksite evaluations, training, and implementation of ergonomic control strategies.
