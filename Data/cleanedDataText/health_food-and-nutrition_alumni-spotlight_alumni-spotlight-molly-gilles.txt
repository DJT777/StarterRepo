Molly Gilles - Alumni Spotlight

    USI Degree: Bachelor of Science in Food and Nutrition- Nutrition and Wellness in 2020
    From: Newburgh, Indiana
    Currently lives in: Nashville, Tennessee

    Current Occupation:
        Operations Manager/Nutritionist at Profile, located in downtown Nashville.
        Works with 40+ clients to help them achieve health goals.
        Specializes in weight management, muscle gain, and maternal nutrition.
        Engages in business development, forming partnerships with different businesses and doctors for referrals.

    Why USI?
        Proximity to home (Newburgh, IN).
        Beautiful campus and friendly atmosphere.
        Formed lasting friendships with roommates.
        Experienced unique opportunities such as body fat tests, cardiovascular fit tests, and food factory tours.
        Enjoyed participating in intramurals with friends.

    How College of Nursing and Health Professions Prepared for Career:
        Nutrition and Wellness degree provided essential knowledge for current career.
        Insight into the restaurant industry and exercise science.
        Appreciates the education received from supportive professors.

    Recommendation for Potential USI Students:
        Highly recommends attending USI.
        Small size provides hands-on help from professors.
        Close proximity to bigger schools for additional experiences.
        No sense of missing out on opportunities.
