University of Southern Indiana (USI) - College of Nursing and Health Professions Graduate Nursing Program

    Programs Offered:

        Online Master of Science in Nursing (MSN)
        Post MSN Advanced Practice Registered Nurse (APRN) Certificate
        Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) - Post MSN and BSN to DNP

    Application Deadline: The deadline for the MSN and Post MSN Certificate programs has been extended to October 15, 2023.

    Clinical Experience: Students will gain extensive clinical nursing experience in various healthcare settings. USI provides exceptional preparation for professional licensing exams.

    Accreditations:
        Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) program is accredited by the Indiana State Board of Nursing.
        Master's degree program in nursing, Doctor of Nursing Practice program, and post-graduate APRN certificate program are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education.
