Employee Assistance Program (EAP):

- The University provides a confidential EAP for eligible employees and members of their household.
- Services provided include assessment, short-term counseling, referral, and follow-up for family, marital, financial, or work-related problems.
- EAP covers up to five free visits, with additional visits covered by most health plans.
- The cost of the EAP is paid by the University.
