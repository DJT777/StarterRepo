    Academic Rights and Appeal Policies:
        - Refer to the University Policy at Academic Integrity and Educational Rights and Privacy Act.
        - The College of Nursing and Health Professions adheres to the standards set in the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act of 1974.
        - Students may request review of their academic file.
        - Students give permission for the college to disclose personal/confidential information to employers and other educational institutions upon admission to the Nursing Program.
