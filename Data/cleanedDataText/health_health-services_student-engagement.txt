Student Organizations in the College of Nursing and Health Professions

    STELLAR ("STudents and ELderly Linking Around Relationships"): Focuses on building positive relationships between students and elders in the community. Activities include visits to elderly residences and hospitals, organizing Christmas parties, and connecting with elderly individuals.
    
    Sigma Phi Omega: National academic honor and professional society in gerontology. Recognizes excellence in the field of aging and provides networking opportunities, leadership roles, and awards of academic excellence. Membership criteria include majoring, minoring, or having an interest in gerontology/aging studies or related fields. Membership application is available online.
