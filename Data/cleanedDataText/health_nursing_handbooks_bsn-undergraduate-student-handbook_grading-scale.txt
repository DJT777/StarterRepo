    Grading Scale for the Undergraduate Nursing Program:

        A = 90-100%
        B+ = 87-89%
        B = 83-86%
        C+ = 80-82%
        C = 75-79%
        D = 70-74%
        F = 69% and below

        *Students must receive a grade of C or greater to pass a course. If a student does not pass a course, their plan of study may need to be revised and progression into the next nursing courses may be delayed. Clinical failures are subject to the determination of the admission and progression committee.
