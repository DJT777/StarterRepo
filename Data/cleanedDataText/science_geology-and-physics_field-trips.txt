Field Trips:

- Importance: Field trips are an essential part of the Geology curriculum at USI.
- Class Field Trips: Geology majors participate in class field trips.
- Extended Summer Field Excursion: Students have the opportunity to be involved in an extended summer field excursion.
- Past Destinations: Yellowstone National Park, the Grand Canyon, Crater Lake, and Big Bend.
- Hands-On Learning: Field trips provide students with important hands-on learning experiences and the opportunity to observe world-renowned geologic sites in person.
- Minimal Expense: Students incur minimal expenses associated with these trips.
