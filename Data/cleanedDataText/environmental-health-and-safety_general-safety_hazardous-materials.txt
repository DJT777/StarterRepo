Hazardous Waste Management Program

    Use of hazardous materials in teaching, research labs, construction, and maintenance operations.
    Proper training required for individuals working with hazardous materials.
    Hazardous Waste Management Plan outlines protocols for collection and disposal of hazardous waste.
    Complete program available on USI's website.

Hazard Communication Program

    Compliance with OSHA's Hazard Communication Standard.
    Chemical inventory maintenance.
    Training provided to employees regarding hazardous chemicals.
    Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) must be easily accessible.
    Labeling requirements for containers of hazardous chemicals.
    Separate OSHA regulation for employees using hazardous chemicals in laboratories.

Biohazard Waste Disposal Program

    Infectious waste boxes with red plastic liners for disposal of research materials.
    Disposal instructions provided.
    Collection containers for sharps.

Used Oil Program

    Proper management and disposal of used oil.
    Definition of used oil and exclusion of specific substances.
    Details available on USI's website.

Mercury-Containing Lamp Program

    Management of waste mercury-containing lamps.
    Compliance with city regulations.
    Details provided on USI's website.

Battery Recycling and Disposal Program

    Proper disposal methods for different types of batteries used on campus.
    Program details available on USI's website.
    Instructions for disposal of batteries not listed provided upon contact with Environmental Health and Safety.

Waste Minimization Plan

    Implementation of waste minimization practices to reduce waste generation.
    Benefits include reducing compliance requirements, costs, and potential environmental liabilities.
    Strategies and techniques provided on USI's website.
