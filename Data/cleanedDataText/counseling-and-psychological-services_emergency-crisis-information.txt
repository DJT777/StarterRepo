Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Emergency / Crisis Information:

- In an Immediately Life-threatening Emergency, Call:
    - On campus: 812-492-7777 (Office of Public Safety)
    - Off campus: 911 or go to the nearest Emergency Room

- Counseling and Psychological Center (CAPS) availability during a crisis:
    - CAPS is available to all students during a crisis, even if already referred to community resources.
    - If you have an urgent or life-threatening situation, please come to or call CAPS and inform front office personnel.
    - DO NOT send an email or leave a message.
