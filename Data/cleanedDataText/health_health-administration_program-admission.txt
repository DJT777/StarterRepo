Bachelor of Science in Health Administration (BSHA) Program Admission

    Program Information:
        College: College of Nursing and Health Professions
        Degree: Bachelor of Science in Health Administration
        Admission Process: No formal application process to declare the BSHA program.
        Major Declaration: Students can declare the major upon entering the university or by completing a change of major form at any time.
        Prerequisites: Completion of prerequisite courses and a minimum GPA of 2.75 prior to starting an internship or Administrator-In-Training (AIT) opportunity.
