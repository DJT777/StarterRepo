Fraternity and Sorority Life at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

    Fraternity and Sorority Life:

        Includes organizations that promote excellence in scholarship, leadership, brotherhood and sisterhood, philanthropy, and personal growth.
        Develops skills in various areas while serving the greater community.
        Emphasizes lifelong learning, leadership, community engagement, and altruism.

    Academic Impact:

        Scholarship is a primary focus within Fraternity/Sorority Life at USI.
        Students are encouraged to excel academically.
        Fraternities and sororities have minimum GPA requirements for membership.

    Social Aspects:

        Fraternities and sororities offer social interactions beyond partying.
        USI ensures a safe and comfortable environment for all participants.
        Strict policies against alcohol on campus and guidelines for events with alcohol.

    Financial Obligation:

        Participation in fraternity or sorority life comes with financial obligations.
        Expect a range of $250 to $400 per semester.
        Fees cover national and chapter expenses, as well as social activities.
        New members may have additional fees for the first semester.

    Hazing Policy:

        USI and individual chapters have strict policies against hazing.
        Hazing is defined as actions creating discomfort, embarrassment, or harassment.
        Hazing is forbidden and will not be tolerated.
        Incidents can be reported to the appropriate office.

    Culturally Based Organizations:

        USI has two historically African American sororities - Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Incorporated and Delta Sigma Theta Sorority Incorporated.
        USI is committed to growing these chapters and adding more NPHC affiliated chapters.

    Governance:

        Each chapter has elected individuals overseeing day-to-day operations and enforcing (inter)national policies.
        Alumni advisors assist with compliance.
        The Program Coordinator for Fraternity/Sorority Life oversees all chapters on campus.
