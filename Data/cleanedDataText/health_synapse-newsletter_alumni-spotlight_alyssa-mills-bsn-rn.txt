Alyssa Mills, BSN, RN - College of Nursing and Health Professions Alumni Spotlight

Profile:
- Inpatient Diabetes Nurse Clinician at Deaconess Gateway
- USI Degree: Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) in 2017, with a minor in Spanish
- From: Evansville, Indiana - Central High School

Achievements:
- 2022 recipient of Nurse of the Year for Community Service award at Deaconess

Reason for Choosing USI:
- Initially interested in pre-medicine, attracted to healthcare programs and the size/class sizes at USI
- Liked that USI was close to home but still gave a sense of going somewhere new
- Beautiful campus

Preparation at the College of Nursing and Health Professions:
- USI prepared Alyssa well for her career as a nurse
- Nursing professors were supportive and wanted students to succeed
- Challenging courses that made learning exciting
- Hands-on experiences during clinicals and projects helped apply classroom knowledge in real-life practice
- Felt confident when taking the NCLEX and starting her first nursing job, knowing she had the knowledge and skills

Involvement at USI:
- Member of the Student Nurse Association

Favorite Memory at USI:
- Class lunch senior year before graduation, gathering with professors and classmates, watching a slideshow of memories and sharing post-graduation plans

Advice for Prospective Students:
- Encourages considering USI, emphasizing the opportunities and experiences the university has to offer
- Appreciative of the education and personal growth she gained at USI
