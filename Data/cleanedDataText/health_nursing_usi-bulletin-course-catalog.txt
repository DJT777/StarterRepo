The University of Southern Indiana Undergraduate Bulletin

Welcome: The bulletin serves as the official source of the University's academic programs, courses, policies, and procedures. It should be used as a guide for course planning and meeting graduation requirements.

Navigation: Use the links in the left-hand menu to navigate through different sections. The search box in the top left corner can be used to search for keywords or phrases in courses, programs, or the entire bulletin. To access the current Graduate Bulletin or Class Schedule, use the drop-down menu in the top right corner. Archived editions of the bulletin or class schedule can be found using the Archived Publications link.

Icon Guide:
- Degree Planner: Provides a print-friendly version of degree requirements in checklist format.
- Add to Portfolio: Allows users to create a personal portfolio using the My Portfolio link.
- Social Media: Share bulletin information on Facebook or Twitter.
- Print-Friendly Format: Generates a pop-up page formatted for neat printing.
- Help: Provides more information on how to use the bulletin.

Contact Information: The bulletin contains contact information for various university offices. If further information is needed, inquiries can be addressed to the appropriate faculty member or university officer. Current information can be found on the university's homepage or specific departments' websites.

Policy Disclaimer: The course offerings and requirements of the University of Southern Indiana are subject to change. This bulletin is not a contractual agreement and the university reserves the right to add, amend, or repeal programs, policies, procedures, regulations, fees, and announcements. The university's Student Rights and Responsibilities: A Code of Student Behavior is subject to change.

Non-Discrimination Policy: The University of Southern Indiana is committed to complying with non-discrimination and equal opportunity laws. Questions or concerns should be directed to the director of Affirmative Action.

Tobacco-Free Campus: The University of Southern Indiana is a tobacco-free campus.

This bulletin is published by the University of Southern Indiana.
