Program Outcomes and Objectives:

Mathematics (B.S./B.A.):
- Employment utilizing mathematical knowledge
- Problem-solving using mathematical knowledge
- Further studies related to mathematics

Mathematics Teaching (B.S./B.A.):
- Employment utilizing mathematical knowledge
- Problem-solving using mathematical knowledge
- Further studies related to mathematics
- Pedagogical knowledge specific to mathematics teaching and learning

Statistics (B.S./B.A.):
- Employment utilizing statistical knowledge
- Problem-solving using statistical knowledge
- Graduate studies related to statistics

Student Learning Outcomes for Mathematics, Mathematics Teaching, and Statistics programs:

- Conceptual Understanding
- Reasoning and Proof (Adaptive Reasoning)
- Algorithmic Thinking (Procedural Fluency)
- Applications (Strategic Competence)
- Communication
- Technology
