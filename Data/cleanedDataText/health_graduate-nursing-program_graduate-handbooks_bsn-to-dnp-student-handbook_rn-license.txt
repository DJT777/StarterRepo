Note:
Students are required to maintain an unencumbered RN license while enrolled and completing the graduate nursing courses. An unencumbered license refers to a license without any suspension, probation, or pending action in any state or U.S. territory of practice. Further information can be found in the Nursing & Health Professions Handbook.
