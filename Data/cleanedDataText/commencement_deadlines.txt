University of Southern Indiana (USI) Commencement Information

Deadlines:

Spring 2024 Commencement:
- Formal Application for Graduation: October 1, 2023
- Reservation to Participate: Beginning February 2, 2024

Fall 2023 Commencement:
- Formal Application for Graduation: March 1, 2023
- Reservation to Participate: Beginning September 12, 2023

Cap and Gown Orders:

- Non-Doctoral: Order online or contact the Campus Store at 812-464-1717.
- Doctoral: Order online or contact the Campus Store at 812-464-1717.

For missed deadlines or questions:

- Cap and gown orders: Contact the Campus Store at 812-464-1717 or via Campus Store email.
