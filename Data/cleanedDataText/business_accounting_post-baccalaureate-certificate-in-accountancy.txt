Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Accountancy - Romain College of Business

    Program Details:
        Accounting jobs continue to be in demand in every industry.
        Designed for those who have already earned a bachelor's degree in a non-accounting major.
        Certificate is viewed by many employers as equivalent to a bachelor's degree in accounting.
        Provides skills to transition to a career in accounting and meet educational requirements to take the CPA exam in Indiana, Illinois, Kentucky, Ohio, and Michigan.
        High job placement rates for graduates.
        Students network with accounting and business professionals on campus and at recruiting events.
        Alumni regularly give back to the College and USI by mentoring students, speaking in classrooms, and attending events.
