DNP Student Virtual Center (DSVC)
    Contact: Health Professions Center 2068, Phone: 812-461-5226, Email provided on the site.

The DSVC is an Internet/Blackboard site that provides comprehensive information for DNP students at the University of Southern Indiana. It includes access to fellow DNP students, employment opportunities, handbooks, advising information, and other resources.
