USI Endeavor Awards For Research & Creativity

Endeavor Symposium:

    Purpose: To encourage, support, and publicize undergraduate research, scholarship, and creativity.
    Required for Endeavor award recipients to present their work.
    Format: Poster or in-person presentation.
    Venue: Carter Hall.
    All undergraduate students, regardless of the use of Endeavor funds, are invited to participate.
    Open to students from universities other than USI.

Important Dates:

    Abstract Submission: February 28 to March 28, 2024.
    Symposium: Thursday, April 11, 2024.

Abstract Submission:

    Online form available on the website.
    Abstracts should be submitted during the specified dates.
