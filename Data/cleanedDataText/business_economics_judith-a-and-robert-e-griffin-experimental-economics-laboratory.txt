Judith A. and Robert E. Griffin Experimental Economics Laboratory:

- Gives undergraduate students the opportunity to participate in economics and management experiments.
- Experiments designed by individual researchers to study behavior in social and business situations.
- Participants can earn cash and contribute to data collection.
- Cash earnings are paid at the end of each session.
