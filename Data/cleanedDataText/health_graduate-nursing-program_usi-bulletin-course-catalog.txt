University of Southern Indiana (USI) 2023-2024 Undergraduate Bulletin

Welcome!

The USI bulletin serves as the official source of information regarding the university's academic programs, courses, policies, and procedures. It should be used as a guide in planning a course of study and meeting graduation requirements.

How to Navigate:

- Use the links in the left-hand menu to navigate through different sections of the bulletin.
- Use the search box in the top left corner to search for keywords or phrases in courses, programs, or the entire bulletin.
- Select the drop-down menu in the top right corner to switch to other publications, such as the Graduate Bulletin or Class Schedule.
- Use the Archived Publications link on the left-hand menu to access older editions of the bulletin or class schedule.

Icon Guide:

- Degree Planner: Provides a print-friendly checklist format of degree requirements.
- Add to Portfolio: Creates a personal portfolio using the My Portfolio link.
- Social Media: Share bulletin information on Facebook or Twitter.
- Print-Friendly Format: Generates a pop-up page formatted for easy printing.
- Help: Provides more information about how to use the bulletin.

Contact Information:

For more specific information or inquiries, contact the appropriate faculty member or university officer. Current information about the university can be found on the USI website, under various departments including:

- Undergraduate Admissions
- Registrar
- Student Financial Assistance
- Bursar
- Dean of Students Office
- Graduate Studies
- Housing and Residence Life

Prospective undergraduate students should contact the Undergraduate Admissions office for more information, schedule a visit, submit an application, or request information.
