Additional Information:

    Graduate nursing clinical practice requirements may be completed in the student's own community.
    Students must be licensed in the state where they are performing clinical.
    Formal application process and affiliation agreement required prior to initiating clinical experiences.
    Preceptors must be board-certified in their specialty.
    No weapons allowed on campus or in clinical areas.
    Clinical hours must begin by Week 3 of a clinical course and continue weekly.
    Up to 10% of clinical hours may be rescheduled with faculty approval.
    Clinical schedules must be provided to clinical faculty.
    Clinical evaluation includes satisfactory achievement according to established criteria.
    Students must pass the clinical portion of a clinical course to pass the course.
