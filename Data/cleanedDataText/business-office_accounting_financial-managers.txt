- Expense account definitions are also available for reference.

Roles and Responsibilities:
- Financial Manager: A full-time employee assigned by the Vice President of Business Affairs.
    - Review financial activity for accuracy and compliance with budget constraints.
    - Ensure financial transactions comply with University guidelines and external restrictions.
    - Approve charges and grant permission for others to view activity in assigned fund/orgs.

- Alternate Financial Manager: Has the same responsibilities and authority as the primary financial manager.

- Accounting Contact: A department's primary liaison with the Accounting Office.
    - Furnish management reports and analyses of revenues and expenses.
    - Assist with questions about transactions and institutional accounting procedures.
    - Process requests related to financial transactions.

- Budget Contact: A department's primary liaison with the Budget Office.
    - Work with departments to develop and maintain annual operating budgets.
    - Assist with questions about budget availability and flexibility.
    - Process requests for budget transfers and reallocations.

- Department Contact: A University employee who serves as the primary contact for the Business Office regarding fund/org-related inquiries and correspondence.
    - Provide information regarding Financial Manager Changes.
    - Submit required financial manager changes to the Accounting Contact, with approval from the current financial manager or supervisor.
