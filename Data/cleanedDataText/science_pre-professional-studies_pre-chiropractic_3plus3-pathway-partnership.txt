Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education - Logan University Chiropractic 3+3 Pathway Partnership

Overview:

    The partnership between USI and Logan University offers a 3+3 pathway program, allowing students to earn a Bachelor of Science in Biology and Doctor of Chiropractic (DC) degrees in just 6 years.
    Students spend 3 years at the University of Southern Indiana and 3 years at Logan University, completing both degrees in a shorter amount of time.

Course of Study:

    Students will follow a 3+3 plan, spending the first 3 years at USI and the next 3 years at Logan University.
    Dedicated advising and collaboration with Logan University ensure a smooth transition and a quality education.

Admission Information:

    For detailed information about the program, including admission requirements and frequently asked questions, visit the 3+3 Pathway Partnership page on the Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education website.
