Information:
- The College of Nursing and Health Professions (CNHP) Advising Center is a part of the University Division at USI.
- The CNHP Advising Center is available to assist students with navigating college life and academic planning.
- Advising services include support in understanding degree requirements, course selection, and career counseling.
- Students can find their assigned advisor's name and contact information through DegreeWorks in their myUSI account.
- Incoming freshmen and "pre" major students are assigned professional academic advisors in the Academic Advising Center.
- Once students are accepted into their program, they will be transitioned to faculty advisors.
- Graduate students and degree completion students should reach out to the Program Chair of their respective program.
- Appointments with advisors can be made using AdvisorTrac via myUSI EagleApps.
- Spring and Summer 2023 registration opens on October 24. Students are encouraged to meet with their advisor before registering.

Additional Information:
- The College of Nursing and Health Professions Advising Center is proud to be a part of the CNHP family and the USI community.
