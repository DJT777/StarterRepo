Prepare for Success:

    Steps to take throughout your college career to set yourself up for success:
        Attend career-related events (job and internship/co-op fairs, career workshops)
        Get involved in extracurricular activities
        Gain career-related experience through volunteer work, summer jobs, internships, or co-op opportunities
        Visit the USI Career Services website
        Network with alumni and professionals in your field of interest

Steps to take in each year:

    First and second year:
        Decide on a major
        Start exploring career opportunities through part-time employment
        Seek career counseling from the USI Career Counseling Office
        Write a polished resume

    Third year:
        Assume leadership responsibilities
        Have a clear career objective
        Consider obtaining an internship or co-op and speak with a Career Services Internship Coordinator

    Graduation year (fall semester):
        Take appropriate graduate/professional school entrance exams
        Identify how positions are filled in your career area of interest
        Update your resume
        Learn to write effective cover letters and other job search correspondence
        Research and make contact with potential employers
        Register with Career Services and Internships
        Check the job posting system
        Prepare for interviews
        Consider obtaining an internship or co-op and speak with the Career Services Internship Coordinator
        Meet with a professional staff member in Career Services and Internships
