Transcript Policy

- Official Transcript: Must be sent directly from the issuing institution to the School of Graduate Studies.
- Unofficial Transcript: Applicant submits a transcript issued to them directly to the School of Graduate Studies.
- International Transcripts: Official International transcripts must be sent from a National Association of Credential Evaluation Services (NACES) member by email.
- Submission of Transcripts: Official transcripts should be sent by email to the School of Graduate Studies.
