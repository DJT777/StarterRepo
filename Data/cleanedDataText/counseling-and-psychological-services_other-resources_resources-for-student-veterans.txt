Resources for Student Veterans

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS):

- CAPS is available to all currently enrolled students, including veterans.
- Student veterans can schedule an appointment by visiting the CAPS office (Orr Center OC 1051) or calling 812-464-1867.

Veteran, Military and Family Resource Center:

- This office aims to assist student veterans in transitioning from military to civilian life.
- Provides guidance for students as they navigate their college experience.
- Assists with applying for and utilizing Department of Veterans Affairs Education benefits.

Additional Treatment Resources:

- Evansville Health Care Center: Located at 6211 E. Waterford Blvd., Evansville, IN 47715. Phone: 812-465-6202.
- Evansville Vet Center: Located at 1100 N. Burkhardt Rd, Evansville, IN 47715. Phone: 812-473-5993 or 812-473-5993.
- Star Behavioral Health Providers: Provides referrals to civilian professionals who have undergone specialized training to work with military service members and their families.
- Military Pathways: Offers free, anonymous mental health and alcohol self-assessments for service personnel and their families. Provides referral information for services through the Department of Defense and Veterans Affairs.

Mental Health Information:

- Department of Veterans Affairs.
- Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration.
- Veterans Crisis Line: Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255), then press 1. Confidential support is available 24/7. Deaf and hard of hearing individuals can also seek support through this line.
