University of Southern Indiana (USI) Athletics

Athletics Department:

- Competitive member of NCAA Division I and the Ohio Valley Conference.
- Men's Soccer and Men's and Women's Swimming and Diving are members of the Summit League.
- Men's Tennis is a member of the OVC/Horizon League partnership.
- Goal of achieving national prominence in each varsity sport while maintaining academic excellence.
- Student athletes maintain an overall GPA of 3.2.
- Nearly 200 Academic All-Conference Awards annually.
- Nationally ranked for student athlete graduation rates.
- Scholarship opportunities available in each varsity sport.
