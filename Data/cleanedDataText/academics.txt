Academics at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

The University of Southern Indiana offers a wide range of academic programs in various fields. They emphasize experiential learning opportunities such as internships, co-ops, study abroad, and community engagement. USI also offers online degree programs.

To search and filter the academic programs, users can visit the USI website.

Academic Programs:

- College of Liberal Arts
- Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education
- Romain College of Business
- College of Nursing and Health Professions
- School of Graduate Studies
- Online Learning
- Outreach and Engagement
- University Division

Students can search for programs by keyword, college, delivery type (on-campus or online), program type, major, minor, and more.

Users can contact USI at 8600 University Blvd or through the provided email address listed on the website.
