University of Southern Indiana (USI) Commencement Information

Ceremony Details for Fall 2023:

- All commencement activities will be held in the Screaming Eagles Arena.
- Doors open to the public 45 minutes before the start of each ceremony.
- No tickets required for guests at this time, but subject to current CDC guidelines and participant numbers.

Ceremony Schedule:

1. School of Graduate Studies (Masters, Post-Masters, and Doctoral degrees related to nursing, health professions, science, engineering, or education)
- Date: Saturday, December 16, 2023
- Ceremony Time: 10:00 a.m.
- Graduate Arrival Time: No later than 9:00 a.m.

2. School of Graduate Studies (Masters and Post-Masters degrees related to liberal arts or business)
- Date: Saturday, December 16, 2023
- Ceremony Time: 1:00 p.m.
- Graduate Arrival Time: No later than 12:00 p.m.
