Conditional Admission to USI Academic Programs

Intensive English Program (IEP)

- Students may apply for undergraduate or graduate admission and admission to the Intensive English Program simultaneously.
- Undergraduate Conditional Admission: Students with good academic or professional qualifications but needing improvement in English language skills may be granted conditional admission without TOEFL or IELTS. Successful completion of the IEP is required to enroll in a major at USI.
- Graduate Conditional Admission: Graduate students can apply as non-degree seeking for the IEP only. A minimum ibtTOEFL score of 79 or a minimum IELTS score of 6 is required for application purposes. Higher scores may be required for specific programs.
