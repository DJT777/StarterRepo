How to Apply for Food and Nutrition Program:

1. Apply for Admission to the University:
   - Complete an admission application and submit to the USI Office of Admissions.
   OR
   - Apply for a Change of Academic Program:
     - Continuing students with at least a 2.0 grade point average may apply for this major and complete a change of academic program form with the Office of the Registrar.

2. Transfer Credits:
   - Submit transcripts from all colleges and universities directly to the Office of the Registrar for evaluation.
   - Transcripts will be reviewed to determine which courses will transfer to USI and their equivalent USI courses.

How to Apply to Dietetics Specialty:

In addition to University admission, students interested in the Dietetics specialty must meet the following requirements and complete the Dietetics Program Application:

- Complete prerequisite courses with a minimum cumulative grade point average of 2.9 (4.0 scale).
- Complete specific course work with a minimum 2.9 overall grade point average:
   - BIOL 121, PSY 201, ENG 101, CMST 101 or 107, MATH 111, CHEM 261, NUTR 203, and NUTR 376.
   - Courses with a "*" require a "C" or better.
