Romain College of Business - Cyber Security Operations Center

The Romain College of Business at the University of Southern Indiana (USI) houses a fully functional Cyber Security Operations Center (SOC). The SOC is operated by student analysts who monitor systems, identify threats, and isolate them to protect the USI campus. This unique opportunity allows students to gain real-world experience while earning money.

Key Features:

1. Gain Experience: Students working in the SOC gain practical experience in monitoring and addressing cyber threats.
2. Make Money: Students are paid for their work in the SOC.
3. Protect the USI Campus: The SOC plays an essential role in safeguarding the USI campus from cyber threats.

Faculty Expertise:

Dr. Kenneth L. Shemroske is the Associate Professor of Computer Information Systems at the Romain College of Business.
