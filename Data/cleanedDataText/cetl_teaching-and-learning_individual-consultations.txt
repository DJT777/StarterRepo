Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning

Services Offered:

- Individual Consultations: The Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning provides one-on-one consultation services to USI faculty.
- Classroom Observations: The center offers classroom observations to provide formative feedback on teaching practices.
- Feedback and Discussion: Consultations include discussions on evidence-based teaching practices and educational research.
- Developing Scholarship of Teaching and Learning (SoTL): Assistance in developing research on teaching and learning.
- Resources: Access to teaching resources and materials.

Requesting Services:

- To schedule an observation or consultation, faculty members can complete the Teaching Observation/Consultation Request Form.
