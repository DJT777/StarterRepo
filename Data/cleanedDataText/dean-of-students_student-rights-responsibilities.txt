University of Southern Indiana (USI) - Code of Student Behavior

Purpose and Application:

- USI's vision statement: To be a recognized leader in higher education, shaping the future and transforming students' lives through exceptional learning and intentional innovation.
- USI's mission statement: An engaged learning community committed to exceptional education, preparing students to lead, make positive contributions to the state and communities, and be lifelong learners in a diverse society.
- Students are responsible for knowing the information, policies, and procedures outlined in this document.
- The code is subject to change, and students should check online for updated versions.

Jurisdiction:

- The code applies to all students enrolled at USI.
- Applies to student conduct both on and off-campus.

Student Rights and Responsibilities:

- Outlines the rights and responsibilities of USI students.

Violations of the Law:

- Addresses student conduct that violates the law.

Academic Integrity:

- Addresses issues related to academic dishonesty and plagiarism.

The Rules:

- Outlines specific rules and expectations for student conduct at USI.

Conduct Process:

- Explains the process for addressing alleged violations of the code.

Student Conduct Authority:

- Identifies the authority responsible for the enforcement of the code.

The Student Rights and Responsibilities document was approved on August 14, 2023, and implemented on August 15, 2023, by Dean Laurie Berry.
