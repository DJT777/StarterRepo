College of Nursing and Health Professions Advising Center

Contact: 
- Location: HP 2023
- Phone: 812-228-5042
- Email provided on the site

Overview: 
- The College of Nursing and Health Professions (CNHP) Advising Center is there to support students in navigating college and adulthood.
- Advisors are available to help, but students are responsible for checking emails and scheduling appointments.
- The advisor's name can be found in DegreeWorks on the myUSI portal.
- Incoming freshmen and "pre" major students are assigned professional academic advisors in the Academic Advising Center.
- Once students are accepted into their program, they are transitioned to faculty advisors.

Appointment Scheduling:
- Students can use AdvisorTrac through myUSI EagleApps to make an appointment with their academic advisor.

Additional Information:
- Registration for Spring and Summer 2023 opens on October 24. It is recommended to meet with an advisor before registering.
- Graduate students and degree completion students should contact the Program Chair of their program for assistance with courses.
