Online MBA Programs at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

- MBA
- MBA with a concentration in Accounting
- MBA with a concentration in Data Analytics
- MBA with a concentration in Engineering Management
- MBA with a concentration in Healthcare Administration
- MBA with a concentration in Human Resources
- MBA with a concentration in Marketing
- MBA with a concentration in Project Management

Program Details:

- Complete coursework in as few as 11 months
- Credit hours: 30
- Tuition: $12,900*

Benefits of the Online MBA Programs at USI:

- Designed to help you take on leadership roles in various businesses
- Affordable and 100% online
- Gain essential tools and professional knowledge for career advancement
- Faculty with a focus on new ideas and collaborative problem-solving
- Choose from multiple high-demand business concentrations

Accreditations:

- The Romain College of Business at USI is accredited by The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB International).

Application Deadlines:

- Next application deadline: 10/6/23
- Start classes: 10/23/23

- Visit the USI website and complete the online application.
