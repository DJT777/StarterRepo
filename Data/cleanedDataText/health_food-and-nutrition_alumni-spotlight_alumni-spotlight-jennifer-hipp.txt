Alumni Spotlight - Jennifer Hipp

- USI Degree: Bachelor of Science in Food and Nutrition, Nutrition and Wellness Minor: Health Promotion and Worksite Wellness, 2014

- Current Occupation: Well-Being Program Manager at WebMD Health Services

- Career Journey: Jennifer initially joined WebMD Health Services in 2014 as a health coach. She later served as an interim Program Manager in 2019 and assumed her current role as a Well-being Program Manager in July 2021. She supports the company's well-being program strategy, planning, and engagement.

- USI Experience: Jennifer chose USI for its inspiring learning culture and opportunities for personal growth. She is grateful for the faculty and advisors who guided her career planning process and helped her form connections. Her time at USI allowed her to obtain a Bachelor's degree in her field of passion and develop lifelong friendships.

- Involvement on Campus: Jennifer participated in the Food and Nutrition Student Organization and played intramural sports. These experiences provided opportunities to connect with like-minded, health-focused students and stay active while meeting new people.

- Favorite Memory: Jennifer fondly remembers her four years at USI, finding the Food and Nutrition program challenging but exciting. She enjoyed learning and growing alongside her colleagues.

- Advice for Prospective Students: Jennifer recently visited USI and was impressed by the campus's growth and expansion. She encourages anyone interested in USI to go for it, as she has no regrets about her decision to attend.
