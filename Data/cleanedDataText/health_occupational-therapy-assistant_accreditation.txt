        Accreditation:

            Accredited by the Accreditation Council for Occupational Therapy Education (ACOTE) of the American Occupational Therapy Association (AOTA).
            Eligibility to sit for the national certification examination administered by the National Board for Certification in Occupational Therapy (NBCOT).
            State licensure may also be required for practice, based on NBCOT results.
            
        Graduation Rates for the Occupational Therapy Assistant Program:

            2022 Cohort: 96% graduation rate
            2021 Cohort: 80.95% graduation rate
            2020 Cohort: 96.56% graduation rate
            2019 Cohort: 96.43% graduation rate
            2018 Cohort: 96.77% graduation rate
            2017 Cohort: 93.33% graduation rate
            Total: 93.90% graduation rate
            
        Certification:

            Graduates are eligible to sit for the national certification examination administered by NBCOT to become a Certified Occupational Therapy Assistant (COTA).
            State licensure is required for practice.
            
        NBCOT Program Results:

            Program results from NBCOT can be found online at https://secure.nbcot.org/data/schoolstats.aspx
