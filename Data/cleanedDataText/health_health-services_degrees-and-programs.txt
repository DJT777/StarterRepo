Student and Program Outcomes:

- Gerontology: Interdisciplinary approach to the study of aging, transmitting and applying gerontological knowledge in healthcare.
- Health Promotion and Worksite Wellness: Obtaining health-related data, distinguishing behaviors that foster well-being, recruiting support for program planning, developing educational programs.
- Public Health: Knowledge and use of biostatistics, understanding the effects of environmental and occupational agents, importance of epidemiology, understanding health services and public health systems, application of social and behavioral disciplines in public health research and practice, addressing healthcare accessibility across populations.
