Advising at USI:

Advising Centers:

Role of Academic Advisors:
    - Help students navigate college life and make informed decisions.
    - Assist with academic planning and course selection.
    - Provide guidance on career options and resources.
    - Support students in achieving their educational goals.

Appointment Scheduling:
    - Access AdvisorTrac through myUSI EagleApps to schedule an appointment with your academic advisor.

Assigned Advisors:
    - Incoming freshmen and "pre" major students are assigned professional academic advisors in the Academic Advising Center.
    - Once accepted into a program, students are transitioned to faculty advisors.
    - Advisor information can be found in DegreeWorks on myUSI.

Additional Resources:
    - Graduate Students and Degree Completion Students: Contact the Program Chair of your program for assistance.
    - Registration for Spring and Summer 2023 opens on October 24. Make sure to meet with your advisor beforehand.
