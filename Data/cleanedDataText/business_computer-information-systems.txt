Computer Information Systems at Romain College of Business

Degrees Offered:

- Major in Computer Information Systems
- Minor in Computer Information Systems
- Minor in Web Development for Enterprise
- Undergraduate Certificate in Computer Information Systems
- Undergraduate Certificate in Cybersecurity
- Undergraduate Certificate in Data Analytics for Business

Why CIS at USI?

- CIS focuses on how computer-based technology improves business efficiency, productivity, and profitability.
- CIS careers offer above-average employment potential, with the field expected to grow twice the national average.
- If you enjoy solving business challenges, CIS is the right fit.
- CIS study includes cybersecurity, data analytics, enterprise web development, and information systems management.
- Small class sizes for individualized attention.
- Dedicated faculty who prioritize teaching and offer open-door policies.

Cybersecurity and Data Analytics:

- USI offers certificate programs in cybersecurity and data analytics.
- Cybersecurity: Learn about information system vulnerabilities and how to prevent and mitigate risks. Includes hands-on experience in the Cybersecurity Operations Center.
- Data Analytics: Emphasizes the application of data analytics concepts for business. Covers visualization, storytelling, machine learning, and data collection and preparation.

Career Opportunities:

- Business Analysts
- Cybersecurity Analysts
- Data Analysts and Data Scientists
- Database Administrators
- Project Management

Accreditation: The Romain College of Business is accredited by The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB International), representing the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide.
