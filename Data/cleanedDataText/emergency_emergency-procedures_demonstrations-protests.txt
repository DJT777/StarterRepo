Demonstrations / Protests:

    Contact: 8600 University Blvd, Phone: 812-464-8600
    Notify USI Public Safety of all demonstrations at 812-492-7777 or extension 7777.
    Public Safety will monitor the situation and contact key administrators as needed.
    Demonstrations should not be obstructed or provoked.
    Non-demonstrators may want to avoid the area.
    University business should continue as normally as possible.
    Conditions that may require contacting Public Safety include disruptions to classes or normal operations, blocked access to facilities or roadways, threats or actual harm to individuals or facilities, unauthorized entry or occupation of university property, or demonstrations within buildings without authorization.
    If a demonstration poses a threat, alert Public Safety, notify individuals in the area, secure doors and equipment, and evacuate if necessary.
    Demonstrations or demonstrators violating university policy or state statutes may be subject to termination of the event, expulsion from campus, or arrest.
