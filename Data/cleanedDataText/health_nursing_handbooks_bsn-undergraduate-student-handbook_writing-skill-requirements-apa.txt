Writing Skill Requirements (APA)

The College of Nursing and Health Professions at the University of Southern Indiana (USI) has specific requirements for writing assignments, following the 7th Edition of the APA Manual.

Guidelines for Adherence to APA Style:

    Faculty will provide guidelines for minimal expectations in adhering to APA style.
    Additional guidance for writing in APA style can be found on the library website.

Nursing & Health Professions Handbook:

    The Nursing Programs at USI abide by the APA style guidelines for written assignments.
    The BSN Undergraduate Student Handbook provides more information on writing skill requirements in the College of Nursing and Health Professions.
