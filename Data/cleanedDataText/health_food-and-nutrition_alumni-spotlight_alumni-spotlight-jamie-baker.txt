    Career Path:

    Involvement at USI:
        Member and President of Food and Nutrition Student Organization (FNSO).

    Favorite Memory at USI:
        Forming lifelong friendships.

    Advice for Prospective Students:
        Encourages anyone to come to USI.
        Provides both traditional college experiences and personal connections with faculty and classmates.
