Department of Biology at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Programs Offered:

- Biology
- Chemistry and Biochemistry
- Engineering
- Geology, Physics, and Environmental Science
- Kinesiology and Sport
- Mathematical Sciences
- Teacher Education
- Pre-Professional Studies

Faculty and Research:

- Faculty members with specializations in various biology fields, including behavioral ecology, botany, entomology, evolution, genetics, cell and molecular biology, physiology, microbiology, and virology.
- Opportunities for students to work closely with faculty on research projects and publish their work in peer-reviewed publications, present at international conferences, and earn competitive scholarships.

- Research opportunities

- Student organizations

Careers and Alumni:

- Biology graduates from USI pursue careers in medicine, research, business and industry, environmental science, healthcare, law, education, wildlife biology, and park services.
- Alumni work for organizations such as Astra Zeneca, Mead Johnson, St. Vincent, Deaconess, ACS Habitat Management, Berry Plastics, Talley Eye Institute, Evansville Vanderburgh School District, and more.

Pre-Professional Programs:

- Bachelor of Medicine (B/MD) program in partnership with Indiana University School of Medicine.
- Pre-Chiropractic program in collaboration with Logan University College of Chiropractic.
- Pre-Medical Laboratory Science with articulation agreements with Good Samaritan and Owensboro Health programs.
- Pre-Pharmacy programs with Sullivan University School of Pharmacy and Manchester University School of Pharmacy.
