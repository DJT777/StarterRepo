    Asbestos:
        Asbestos-containing material defined as any material containing more than one-percent asbestos.
        Asbestos used in various building construction products.
        USI follows regulations of OSHA, EPA, and the state of Indiana for asbestos removal.
        USI has an Asbestos Management Program and policy in place.

    Mold:
        Mold removal requires addressing the cause of moisture and mold contaminants.
        USI follows guidelines from the New York City Department of Health & Mental Hygiene.
        Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) provides technical assistance and develops a mold abatement strategy.
        EHS assists with abatement priorities, contracts, supervision, monitoring, waste disposal, and compliance.
        USI has a Mold Management Program to address mold in university buildings.
