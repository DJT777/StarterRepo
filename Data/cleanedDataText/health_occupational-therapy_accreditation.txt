College of Nursing and Health Professions at the University of Southern Indiana

Occupational Therapy

    Program Accreditation:
        Accredited by the Accreditation Council for Occupational Therapy Education (ACOTE) of the American Occupational Therapy Association (AOTA).
        ACOTE contact information: 6116 Executive Boulevard, Suite 200 North Bethesda, MD 20852-4929, Phone: (301) 652-AOTA, Website: https://acoteonline.org/

    Certification and Licensure:
        Graduates eligible to sit for the national certification examination for occupational therapists administered by the National Board for Certification in Occupational Therapy (NBCOT).
        Most states require licensure for practice, usually based on NBCOT exam results.
        Note that a felony conviction may affect eligibility for certification and licensure.

    Graduation Rates:
        Period: 2020-2022.
        Total graduates: 88.
        Overall graduation rate: 98%.
        
    Level II Fieldwork:
        Must be completed within 24 months following the completion of the didactic portion of the program.
        
    Notice regarding felony convictions:
        Felony convictions, including DUI, can impact eligibility for certification and state licensing.
        Students are advised to inform the Occupational Therapy Program promptly if they have a felony conviction or if their credentials have been denied, revoked, suspended, or subject to probationary conditions in another field.
