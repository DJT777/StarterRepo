Purpose of the Athletics Council:
- The purpose of the University Athletics Council (UAC) is to provide oversight for USI intercollegiate athletics, support the development of excellence in USI intercollegiate athletics, and serve as an advisory council to the President.
- The functions of the UAC include overseeing intercollegiate athletics, monitoring trends in GLVC and NCAA bylaws/decisions, providing input into the role of intercollegiate athletics at USI, and delivering an annual data dashboard on intercollegiate athletics trends.

USI Athletics Inclusion Statement:
- USI Athletics embraces individuals of all races, sexes, religions, abilities, ages, national origins, veteran statuses, sexual orientations, and gender identities.
- They support equitable practices to increase opportunities for all members of the community and actively invite all individuals to participate in athletics.
