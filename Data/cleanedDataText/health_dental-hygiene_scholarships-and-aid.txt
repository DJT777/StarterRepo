    Dental Hygiene Scholarships:

        Indiana Dental Hygienist's Association Ohio Valley Component Scholarship ($400)
            Criteria: Awarded to a junior dental hygiene student who is an active member of the ADHA USI Student Chapter, meets ADHA Code of Ethics, has a minimum overall GPA of 3.5, and demonstrates excellent clinical aptitude.

        Lloyd R. Wallis Memorial Scholarship ($1000)
            Criteria: Awarded to a dental hygiene student with a preference for a student in need. Not awarded to graduating seniors. Requires enrollment during the upcoming academic year and can be renewed with a 3.0 GPA.

        Phyllis Rains Babb Dental Hygiene Memorial Scholarship Endowment ($1000)
            Criteria: Awarded to full-time USI students who are natural-born citizens of the United States majoring in dental hygiene, with preference given to students from the Tri-State area (Indiana, Illinois, and Kentucky). Requires good academic standing and a 3.0 GPA or higher.

        Contact Dr. Kimberly M. Hille, Assistant Dean of Health Professions and Associate Professor of Dental Hygiene and Dental Assisting, for more information on applying for these scholarships.
