Panhellenic Sorority Recruitment at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Formal Recruitment Process:

    Registration: Closes at NOON on September 13, 2023. Registration can be done online.
    
        Registration Eligibility:
            Must be enrolled as a full-time, degree-seeking student at USI.
            Most chapters have a minimum GPA requirement of 2.5 (some sororities have a higher requirement).
            Pay a non-refundable $30 recruitment registration fee.
            Plan to attend all recruitment events.
            
    Recruitment Events: September 15-17, 2023
    
        Develop in Sisterhood Round: September 15, 2023, 4:30 PM to 11:10 PM
            Open house-style event where PNMs meet each participating sorority for a 40-minute conversation.
        
        Philanthropy Round: September 16, 2023, 12 PM to 7 PM
            PNMs can visit up to four parties, each lasting 40 minutes, to learn about the philanthropic endeavors of the sororities.
        
        Preference Round: September 17, 2023, 8:30 AM to 1:30 PM
            PNMs visit up to two events, lasting 60 minutes each. Sorority members explain what it means to be a member of their sorority and what they can offer.

        Bid Day: September 17, 2023, Arrive by 5 PM
            PNMs may receive up to one bid (invitation to join) from a sorority. Accepting the bid means becoming a new member of that chapter.
