Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education: Advising Information

Advising:

- Students are advised by full-time engineering faculty throughout their tenure at the University. Faculty advisors are available during the academic year to discuss goals, career alternatives, and progress.
- Close collaboration between students and advisors contributes to success.
- Student Advising and Resource Center: Available to help first-year students, transfer students preparing to be admitted to their major, and Pott College students seeking academic and career advising.
