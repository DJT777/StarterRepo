Distribution Services at University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Forms and Service Requests

Distribution Services provides various services related to mail, shipments, warehousing, and fulfillment. The following forms can be used to request specific services:

1. Mail Processing Order: Complete this form to request services described on the Mail Preparation & Processing Services page.

2. Fleet Copy Paper Requests: Use this form to fulfill regular copy paper requests for the fleet copiers.

3. Freight Delivery Request: This form should be completed after receiving a Freight Received Notice. It is used to provide instructions for the delivery or storage of freight shipments received by Distribution Services.

4. Distribution Materials Request Form: Use this form to order mailing envelopes, packaging materials, bulk mail containers, and campus mail supplies from UPS, FedEx, and USPS.

5. Storage, Transfer, Repurpose, or Disposal Request Form: Complete this form when requesting the repurposing, disposal, storage, or transfer of items. Attach the confirmation to the work order submission.
