Homecoming 2024

The Center for Campus Life at the University of Southern Indiana is excited to announce Homecoming 2024!

Save the Dates: Homecoming 2024 will take place in February 2024.

Event Details: The Center for Campus Life is planning a series of events and activities to celebrate Homecoming. The campus will be filled with school spirit, and there will be opportunities to support the athletic teams.

Connect with USI: Stay updated on Homecoming 2024 and other university news through social media channels: Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

Contact: For more information, contact the Center for Campus Life at University Center East, UC 0235. Phone: 812-228-5107, Email provided on the site.
