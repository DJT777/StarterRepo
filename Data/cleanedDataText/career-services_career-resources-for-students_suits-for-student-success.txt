    Suits for Student Success:
        Provide professional business attire for students.
        Students can choose one free outfit per calendar year from the Suits for Student Success Closet.
        Outfit includes slacks or skirt, blouse or shirt, jacket, and shoes.
        Limited availability of sizes and styles.
        Students can keep the clothing for use in interviews or future jobs.
        Donations of clean, high-quality interview clothes and shoes are appreciated.
        Donations can be dropped off or contact Career Services and Internships for arrangements.
        Discount offers from local business attire stores are also welcomed.

    Suits for Student Success Closet Hours:
        Monday - Friday: 9:00 a.m. - 3:30 p.m.
        Closed on school holidays
