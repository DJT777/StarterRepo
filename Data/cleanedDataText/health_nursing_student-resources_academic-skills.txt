Academic Skills at University Division

Academic Support Structure:

- Academic Skills offers several support structures to help students achieve their educational goals at USI.
- They provide a relaxing, friendly, and non-judgmental environment for students.
- On-site subject-based tutors are available to provide assistance.
- Supplemental Instruction (SI) is available.
- Writing help is offered.
- Academic coaching and study skills assistance are provided.
- Programs, such as Student Success Week, are developed in collaboration with other departments to promote student achievement.

General Studies Courses:

- Academic Skills also offers General Studies courses (GENS) that focus on helping students develop academically.

Joining the Team:

- Academic Skills hires students for various positions, including peer tutors, SI leaders, and front desk workers.
- Interested students can fill out an application and submit it to the office or by email.
