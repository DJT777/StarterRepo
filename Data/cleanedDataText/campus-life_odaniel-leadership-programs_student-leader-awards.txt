Center for Campus Life Student Leader Awards

- Profile
    - The University of Southern Indiana and Center for Campus Life recognizes outstanding student leaders.
    - Annual ceremony held in Carter Hall.
    
- Individual Student Leadership Awards Application
    - Application open.
    - Application deadline: 11:59pm on March 19, 2023.
    
- Award Categories:
    - Top Student Leader: Presented to ten students who have made a significant impact through their leadership in campus organizations. Must be currently enrolled at USI with a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.75.
    - All Campus Achievement: Awarded to two students who have completed seventy-five or more credit hours at USI with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.00. Selection based on academic achievement, service to the community, and participation in co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

- Recognition:
    - Recipients' names added to a perpetual plaque displayed outside Traditions Lounge in University Center.
