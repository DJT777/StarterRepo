Student Experiences:

Student 1 - Kelsey Likens
  - Hometown: Boonville, Indiana
  - Major: Electrical Engineering
  - Enjoyed math and had an interest in computer science, which led her to pursue engineering.
  - Chose USI for its campus atmosphere and proximity to the city.
  - Joined the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Robotics Club and participated in competitions.
  - Found plenty of opportunities for hands-on experience outside the classroom, such as helping with a robotics competition for middle schools.
  - Appreciated the support of the engineering faculty and their expertise.
  - Aspires to make systems more efficient and would like to have a career with global travel opportunities.

Student 2 - Wyatt Helms
  - Hometown: Alfordsville, Indiana
  - Major: Mechanical and Electrical Engineering
  - Comes from a family of engineers and enjoyed problem-solving.
  - Chose USI because of its strong engineering program and proximity to home.
  - Received a USI Presidential Scholarship.
  - Highlight of his time at USI was being part of the UNITE CubeSat team, designing and building a satellite for NASA.
  - Emphasized the importance of teamwork in engineering and praised the faculty.
  - Already secured a full-time position as an engineer with Crane Naval Base, working in antenna design and testing.
