Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning (CETL)

    About CETL
        Mission: Foster excellence in all areas of faculty work and support the success of the faculty and academic staff at the University of Southern Indiana (USI).
        Values:
            Fostering inquiry and learning.
            Using impact-driven and evidence-based practices.
            Serving with excellence and integrity.
            Providing support.
            Facilitating connections.
            Nurturing inclusive environments.
        Goals:
            Develop and provide programming to enhance student learning through evidence-based teaching practices.
            Support faculty in their academic goals through professional development.
            Document the impacts of faculty work.
            Foster connections and community.
            Foster a culture of inclusion.
            Facilitate continuous improvement of CETL programs through evaluation.
