Scholarships and Aid in Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education:
- Pott Engineering Scholarships are awarded to students interested in the engineering career field and with demonstrated academic ability.
- Various scholarships and their criteria are listed, such as the Alcoa Foundation Endowed Engineering Scholarship, Biagi Chance Cummins London Titzer Endowed Engineering Scholarship, Bob & Carole Rust Engineering Scholarship, and more.
- Each scholarship has specific criteria for selection, number of awards, and award amounts.
- Scholarships may have renewable or non-renewable criteria, and certain scholarships may require validation of financial need from the Student Financial Assistance office.

Guidelines for Pott Engineering Scholarship:
- Eligibility: High school GPA of 3.5 or better, SAT score of 1200 or ACT score of 27 (for entering freshmen); minimum high school GPA of 3.5 (for full-time students); minimum college transfer GPA of 3.0 (for transfer students).
- Scholarship value: $1,500 per academic year (fall and spring).
- Scholarship renewal: Automatic renewal if full-time enrollment, continued major in engineering, and minimum 3.0 USI cumulative GPA after two semesters of enrollment.
- Applications: Apply for admission to USI as an Engineering major by March 1. Scholarship applications available from the Student Financial Aid Office.
