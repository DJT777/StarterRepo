The Archie Mascot Program at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

    Be Archie/Archie's Friend:

        Purpose: Represent USI at athletic and community events as primary spirit role models and ambassadors.
        Goal: Inspire Screaming Eagles and support the university's goals of achieving national prominence in sports and academic excellence.

    Archie:

        Role:
            Attend required campus, athletic, and community events.
            Generate crowd involvement, socialize with, and entertain fans.
            Encourage Screaming Eagle pride.
        Scholarship: $2,500 annual scholarship applied to the 2020-2021 academic year.
        Commitment: 3-15 hours per week, 70-95 hours per semester.
        Requirements:
            Full-time student at USI.
            Good academic standing with a GPA of 3.0 or higher on a 4.0 scale.
            Preferred height between 5'4" and 5'11".

    Understudy Archie:

        Role:
            Attend required campus, athletic, and community events.
            Generate crowd involvement, socialize with, and entertain fans.
            Encourage Screaming Eagle pride.
            Coordinate with Archie to create a cohesive schedule.
        Scholarship: $1,500 annual scholarship applied to the 2020-2021 academic year.
        Opportunity for advancement to primary Archie position.
        Commitment: Hours vary weekly, 45-70 hours per semester.
        Requirements:
            Full-time student at USI.
            Good academic standing with a GPA of 3.0 or higher on a 4.0 scale.
            Preferred height between 5'4" and 5'11".

    Archie's Friends:

        Role:
            Assist Archie at events as needed.
            Guide Archie, talk for Archie, pass out promotional items, etc.
            Attend events as Archie if needed (mostly for photo opportunities).
        Compensation: Hourly pay.
        Commitment: Hours vary.
        Requirements:
            Full-time student at USI.

    Contact: 8600 University Blvd, Phone: 812-464-8600, Email provided on the site.
    Social Media Channels: Facebook, YouTube, Instagram.
