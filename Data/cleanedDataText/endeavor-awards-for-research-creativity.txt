Endeavor Awards For Research & Creativity

Purpose: The Endeavor Awards aim to support the creative and research efforts of undergraduate students from all majors at USI.

About the Awards:

1. Student Collaboration Award:
   - Purpose: To help defray costs associated with research or creative projects.
   - Allowable costs: Lab work, equipment, materials, and travel to research sites.
   - Application: Students write proposals with assistance from faculty mentors.
   - Funding: Students can apply for up to $2,500.
   - Deadline:
     - Fall: Friday, October 6, 2023
     - Spring: Friday, February 9, 2024
   - Submission: Only electronic submissions will be accepted.
   - Requirement: Proposals must have a substantial student research or creative component.

2. Student Presentation Award:
   - Purpose: To assist with travel costs for presenting a project at an off-campus professional meeting.

3. Endeavor Symposium:
   - Purpose: A symposium held on the USI campus each April for presenting student research and creative projects.
   - Participation: Obligatory for students receiving Collaboration Awards and/or Presentation Awards.
   - Abstract Submission: USI undergraduate and graduate students can submit abstracts for consideration.
   - External Participation: Students from universities other than USI may also submit abstracts for consideration.
