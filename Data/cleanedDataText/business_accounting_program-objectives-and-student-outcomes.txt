Program Objectives and Student Outcomes:

Accounting:

- Cost/Managerial: Applying concepts and skills in product costing, budgeting, and performance analysis.
- Financial: Preparing and interpreting financial statements, analyzing company performance, and applying GAAP to financial transactions.
- Taxation: Understanding federal income tax law, explaining sources and rationale for tax law, identifying requirements for business deductions, and differentiating between realized and recognized gains and losses.
- Audit: Applying auditing standards, employing appropriate audit procedures, and recognizing professional behavior expectations of auditors.
- Ethical Decision Making: Demonstrating ethical decision making relevant to the accounting profession.
- Analytical Problem Solving: Demonstrating analytical problem solving skills inherent in the accounting profession.
