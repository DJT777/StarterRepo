Career Services and Internships

Career Resources for Students:
- Provides resources and guidance for students regarding their career development.
- Offers assistance in resume and cover letter writing, interview preparation, and job search strategies.

Internships and Co-Ops:
- Helps students find internships and cooperative education opportunities to gain practical work experience in their field of study.

Career Fairs and Events:
- Organizes career fairs and events where students can connect with potential employers and explore job opportunities.

Career Launch/Symplicity:
- Provides access to Career Launch, an online platform that connects students with internship and job opportunities.
- Symplicity is a comprehensive career services management system used by students and alumni.

Information for Employers:
- Provides information and resources for employers interested in recruiting USI students and graduates.

Information for Faculty/Staff:
- Offers support and resources for faculty and staff to assist students in their career development.

Career Ready Competencies:
- Identifies the essential skills and competencies that students need to possess for career success.

Career Outcomes:
- Provides information and data on the career outcomes of USI graduates.
- Offers a data dashboard that includes employment data, such as employer information, job titles, salaries, and graduate school information.

Frequently Asked Questions:
- Answers common questions students may have about career services and internships.
