    Teach Now-Transform Tomorrow Program
        Clinical Internship Program for education majors
        Partnership with the Evansville Vanderburgh School Corporation
        Fall and spring semester placement in EVSC schools
        Assigned to a cooperating teacher
        Participate in all professional development and faculty meetings
        Responsibilities grow from observations to planning, instruction, assessments, and parent communication
        Supported by Teach Now-Transform Tomorrow faculty advisor and Director of Clinical Internships
        Financial support through scholarships
        Applications submitted in January for the following academic year
