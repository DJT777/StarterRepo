Course Delivery:
The BSN to DNP courses will follow a hybrid model of course delivery, combining on-campus education with distance education using the Blackboard course management system. Students are required to have a computer with internet access that supports video streaming. On-campus course sessions will be held 2-3 days, dedicated to class time, presentations, student projects, DNP projects, and faculty meetings. Attendance for each on-campus session is mandatory.
