Geology Program at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

- Program Overview:
    - Geology combines aspects of biology, earth science, chemistry, and physics.
    - Provides analytical, critical thinking, and communication skills.
    - Offers opportunities for hands-on research, conference presentations, and field trips to geological locales.
    - Departmental scholarships and internships available.
    - Geology is listed as the 7th most valuable college major by Forbes magazine.
    - Geology graduates can pursue careers in academia, business, consulting, education, engineering, government, mineral exploration, and the petroleum industry.
