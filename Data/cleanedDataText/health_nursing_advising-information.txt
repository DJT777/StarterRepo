Advising Information:

- All incoming freshmen and "pre" major students are assigned professional academic advisors within the Academic Advising Center.
- Once students are accepted into their program, they transition to faculty advisors.
- Graduate students and degree completion students should contact the Program Chair of their program for assistance.
- To make an appointment with an academic advisor, students can use AdvisorTrac through their myUSI EagleApps.
- Registration for Spring and Summer 2023 opens on October 24. Students are advised to meet with their advisors before registering.

Important News:

- Students are encouraged to log into myUSI to access AdvisorTrac for appointments with their advisor.
