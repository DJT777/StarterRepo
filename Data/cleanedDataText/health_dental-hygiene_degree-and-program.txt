The Dental Hygiene Program at the University of Southern Indiana provides a comprehensive curriculum that includes didactic courses, laboratory and clinical experiences, and community oral health education experiences. Graduates of the program will be prepared to deliver oral health care services and education in various practice settings. The curriculum is delivered in state-of-the-art facilities and utilizes a variety of learning resources and methods.

Core 39

- In addition to departmental degree requirements, undergraduate students must complete the University Core 39.
- The core provides a foundation for all baccalaureate degrees at the University of Southern Indiana.
- It prepares students to live wisely in a diverse and global community by acquiring discipline-specific competence and broader knowledge and skills that cut across disciplines.
