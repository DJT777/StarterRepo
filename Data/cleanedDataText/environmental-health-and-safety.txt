    Role and Responsibilities:
        The Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) office ensures a safe and healthy environment for students, faculty, staff, and visitors.
        Develop and implement policies, procedures, and programs to promote occupational health, safety, and environmental compliance.
        Conduct regular inspections and provide training and guidance on safety practices.
        Oversee hazardous waste management, chemical safety, emergency preparedness, and other related areas.
        Fosters a culture of safety and sustainability on campus.
