Bursar's Office

About Us:
The Bursar's Office at the University of Southern Indiana provides services related to tuition and fees, billing and payments, and non-student accounts receivable.

Services Offered:

Tuition and Fees: Information about the cost of attendance and payment options.

Billing and Payments: Guidance on how to pay tuition and fees.

Forms: Various forms related to financial matters, including tax forms and deferment forms.

Non-Student Accounts Receivable: Information and assistance for non-student accounts.

Financial Care Team: Support for students experiencing financial difficulties.

Frequently Asked Questions: Answers to common queries about financial matters.

Contact Information: Lower level of the Orr Center, Phone: 812-464-1842, Email provided on the site.

Forms Available for Students:

Tax Form: Form W-9S for requesting a student or borrower's taxpayer identification number and certification.

Employer Deferment Forms: For students with tuition reimbursement from their employers to defer their tuition bill. Employer Deferment Form available.

Tuition Itemization Letter Request Form: Request form for a letter itemizing tuition expenses.

Miscellaneous Forms: Includes forms for agency account deposits, bookstore charge limit exceptions, and opting out of the First Day Access eBook program.

Social Media Channels: Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram.

Contact Information: 8600 University Blvd, Evansville, IN 47712, Phone: 812-464-8600, Email provided on the site.
