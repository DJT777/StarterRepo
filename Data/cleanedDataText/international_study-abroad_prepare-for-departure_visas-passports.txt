Visas & Passports

Passports:
- U.S. citizens need a passport to travel outside the United States.
- Visit the U.S. Department of State website for more information.
- Apply for a passport as early as possible, as processing can take four to six weeks.
- The total cost for a passport is $135, with an additional $60 for expedited processing.
- Passport applications are available at the Center for International Programs (CIP) office or the USPS Passport Agent.
- An appointment is required for the USPS Passport Agent.
- Return the completed application with proof of citizenship, proof of identity, and two passport-ready photos.
- Ensure that your existing passport will not expire while you are abroad.

Visas:
- Some countries may require a student visa, which should be obtained in advance.
- Consult the embassy website of your host country for specific requirements.
- In some cases, such as Spain, the visa application must be done in person at the consulate.
- In other cases, like China, the visa can be obtained through an approved travel agency.
- Apply for your visa well in advance to avoid last-minute issues.
