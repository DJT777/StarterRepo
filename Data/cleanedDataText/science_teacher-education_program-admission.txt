Admission Requirements for Undergraduate Teacher Education:

- Admission process confirms intent to pursue teacher licensure at USI in an education major or minor program.
- Access to advanced education courses restricted to formally admitted teacher candidates.
- Requirements for admission:
  - University cumulative GPA of 2.75 or higher.
  - Meet the Academic Achievement Requirement for Teacher Preparation Programs.
  - Application submitted using the Tk20 system in myUSI.
  - Recommendation of major department advisor.
  - Acceptable criminal background checks.
  
Undergraduate Academic Achievement Requirements for Teacher Preparation Programs:

- Undergraduate cumulative GPA of 2.75 or higher.
- Demonstrate successful completion of communication, composition, and mathematics requirements in the Foundation Skills Section of CORE 39.
- Successful completion defined as earning a C or higher in relevant courses.
- Acceptable performance test scores may also be considered.
