Asbestos Fiber Release Procedure

    Contact Information:
        Location: 8600 University Blvd
        Phone: 812-464-8600
        Email: provided on the site

    Procedure:

        Accidental asbestos fiber releases are uncommon at USI, but there is a potential for such incidents.
        
        In the event of a release, the Environmental Health and Safety department will:
            Assess and coordinate emergency isolation, cleanup, and air monitoring activities.
        
        If an asbestos release occurs within a building:
            The affected area will be evacuated and sealed off.
            HVAC inlets and outlets to the area will be isolated.
            All doors and windows in the area will be closed.
        
        If you encounter or suspect an asbestos fiber release:
            Contact Physical Plant at 812-464-1782.
