College of Nursing and Health Professions - Alumni Spotlight: Kelsey Bruner

Meet Kelsey Bruner, MS, RD, LD, a long-term care consultant Registered Dietitian with American Senior Communities. She graduated from the University of Southern Indiana (USI) with a Bachelor of Science in Food and Nutrition - Dietetics in 2019.

Some details about Kelsey Bruner:

- Hometown: Boonville, Indiana
- Current Residence: Evansville, Indiana
- Occupation: Long-Term Care Consultant Registered Dietitian with American Senior Communities

How USI and the College of Nursing and Health Professions prepared Kelsey for her career:

- Hands-on counseling opportunities to develop nutrition education and counseling skills.
- Creating meals from different cultures, teaching multi-tasking, teamwork, and time management.
- Learning about medications, diseases, and how to treat them with nutrition.
- Confidence gained through the dietetic internship and learning opportunities.

Involvement at USI:

- Activities Programming Board as an assistant programming chair.
- Student worker in the Admissions Office.
- Student Ambassador giving weekly tours to students and families.

Favorite Memory at USI:

- Thankful for opportunities in food and nutrition-related activities and other programs.
- Discovering other skills and developing rapport through extracurricular activities and jobs.

Advice for prospective USI students:

- Seek out and take any opportunities that come your way.
- Build good relationships with professors and peers.
- Make connections in the community for potential future opportunities.
