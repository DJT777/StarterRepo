University of Southern Indiana (USI) College of Nursing and Health Professions

Nursing Programs:
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)
- RN to BSN
- Graduate Nursing Programs (Master of Science in Nursing, Doctor of Nursing, Post MSN certificates)

Program Highlights:
- Multiple program options available, including online options.
- High-quality education at an affordable price.
- Smaller class sizes and a supportive learning environment.
- Expert faculty with advanced nursing degrees and certifications.
- Preparation for roles in advanced practice nursing, leadership, public health, academia, and more.

Nursing Program Information:
- Accredited by the Indiana State Board of Nursing and the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE).
- Various majors and program types available, such as Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner, Family Nurse Practitioner, Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, Nursing Education Leadership, and more.
- Strong employment prospects with a projected job growth of 6% through 2031.
- Median annual salary of $77,600 (BLS 2023).
- 100% NCLEX pass rate in May 2023 (well above national rate).

Additional Resources:
- Nursing handbooks, academic practice partnerships, student resources, and organizations.
- USI Foundation scholarships for nursing students.
- Facilities and services available to nursing students.
- Information on the NCLEX exam and program outcomes.
