Congratulations on your acceptance to the University of Southern Indiana (USI)!

Next Steps for Admitted Students:

Step 1: Submit Enrollment Deposit - Accept your admissions offer by submitting the required enrollment deposit.
Step 2: Submit Immigration Documents - Complete the necessary immigration documentation.
Step 3: Activate Your myUSI Student Portal - Activate your myUSI portal to access important resources and information.

Orientation and Arrival Information:

If you have completed the above steps and are ready for orientation and arrival details, review the information for international student orientation.

Declining Admissions Offer:

If you have been admitted to USI but do not plan to attend, please complete the USI Withdrawal Form. This form is for students who applied to USI but have changed their minds.

Further Engagement with USI:

Stay connected with USI through our social media channels: Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.
