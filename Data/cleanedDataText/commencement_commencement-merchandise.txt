Commencement Information

Merchandise:

Caps and Gowns: Regalia can be ordered online through the USI Campus Store or in person for same-day pickup. Doctoral graduates should contact the USI Campus Store directly.
Formal Announcements: Announcements can be ordered online through a provided link. Payment can be made with a PayPal account or credit card.

Photography:

GradImagesÂ®: Photos of each graduate will be taken during the ceremony. Approximately one week after the event, proofs will be emailed to graduates for review.

Gifts and Memorabilia:

USI Alumni Council: Commencement gifts and memorabilia, including floral bouquets, t-shirts, and sweatshirts with all graduate names, will be available for purchase outside the Screaming Eagles Arena. Preordering is available at www.thecommencementgroup.com/uofsouthernindiana.
Celebrate!: Customized yard signs for USI 2023 graduates can be purchased from the USI Copy Center. Orders can be placed online and must be picked up at the USI Publishing Services Center.
