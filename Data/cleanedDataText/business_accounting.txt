Romain College of Business - Accounting Program

Overview:

- USI's accounting program is highly regarded and offers a competitive advantage in the job market.
- Accounting is considered the language of business, and individuals fluent in this language are in high demand.
- The program aims to provide exceptional learning experiences and produce professionally trained accountants for regional and national employers.
- USI accounting students have excelled in national and international business case competitions.
- The accounting program holds special accreditation from the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), a mark of top-quality accounting programs in the country.
- USI ranks in the top five for Certified Management Accountant (CMA) exam success.

Programs Offered:

- Major in Accounting and Professional Services
- Major in Accounting and Professional Services with Taxation Emphasis
- Major in Accounting and Professional Services with Investment Accounting Emphasis
- Minor in Accounting
- Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Accountancy
