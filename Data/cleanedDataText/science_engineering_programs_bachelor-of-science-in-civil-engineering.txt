Civil Engineering Program at USI

- Civil Engineering is a fast-growing field focused on solving problems in infrastructure, such as roads, bridges, dams, and structures.
- USI's Engineering Program has a strong reputation and prepares graduates for success in industry.
- State-of-the-art labs and hands-on learning opportunities through design challenges and competitions.
- Small class sizes and experienced faculty who are licensed professional engineers.
- Unique opportunities to tackle real-world engineering challenges, including internships, co-op programs, and student competition teams.
- BSCE-specific courses covering various subfields of civil engineering.
- Applied Engineering Center equipped with custom-made manufacturing and engineering equipment.
- Graduates are in demand and qualified for diverse career paths in both private and public sectors.

For more information on the Civil Engineering Program at USI, contact USI Admissions.
