Pharmacy Pathway Partnership with Sullivan University's Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education

    Overview:
        University of Southern Indiana (USI) students have the opportunity to pursue a 6-year dual degree program in partnership with Sullivan University's College of Pharmacy and Health Services.
        This program allows students to earn a Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.) degree from Sullivan University and a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology, Biochemistry, or Chemistry from USI in a timelier and more cost-effective manner than traditional pathways.
    
    Requirements:
        Complete 90 hours of coursework at USI in the Biology, Biochemistry, or Chemistry degree.
        Complete the application process and be accepted into Sullivan University's College of Pharmacy and Health Services.
        Complete specific PharmD courses at Sullivan University and provide the transcript to USI.
    
    Benefits:
        Students can earn both their undergraduate and PharmD degrees in as few as six years.
        This pathway allows students to enter the workforce as pharmacists one to two years earlier than traditional PharmD programs.
