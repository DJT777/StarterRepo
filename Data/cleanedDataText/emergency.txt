Public Safety

- Public Safety office maintains 24-hour coverage of the campus.
- Responsibilities include traffic control, security of physical assets, and safety of employees, students, and guests.
- Security staff are trained in first aid and emergency procedures.
- Emergency phones located throughout university buildings and elevators.
- Contact: Security Building, 812-464-1845 (routine business), extension 7777 (emergencies).

Staying Connected

- USI Rave Alert: Critical and timely warnings via email, text message, and voice message about emergencies, severe weather, and incidents impacting the university community.
- Everyone with a USI email address is automatically enrolled.
- USI Public Safety manages Rave Guardian, an app-based tool for contacting Public Safety and monitoring safety during travel.
