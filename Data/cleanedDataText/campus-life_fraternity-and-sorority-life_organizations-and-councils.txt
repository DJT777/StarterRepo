Center for Campus Life - Fraternity and Sorority Life

The Center for Campus Life at the University of Southern Indiana serves as the center of programming and advising for the fraternity and sorority community. They aim to assist students in developing into responsible, social, and contributing members of society. The fraternity and sorority community at USI consists of chapters of inter/national fraternities and sororities.

Councils:

1. Panhellenic Council (PHC): Provides educational opportunities, governance, accountability, and structure for the sorority chapters at USI.
   - Alpha Sigma Alpha
   - Alpha Sigma Tau
   - Delta Zeta
   - Gamma Phi Beta
   - Sigma Sigma Sigma

2. Interfraternity Council (IFC): Provides educational opportunities, governance, accountability, and structure for the fraternity chapters at USI.
   - Kappa Alpha Order
   - Lambda Chi Alpha
   - Sigma Pi
   - Sigma Tau Gamma
   - Tau Kappa Epsilon

National Pan-Hellenic Council (NPHC): There are two active chapters associated with NPHC, but there is currently no NPHC Governing Council at USI.
   - Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Inc.
   - Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.

National Association of Latino Fraternal Organizations, Inc. (NALFO): There is one active chapter associated with NALFO, but there is currently no NALFO Governing Council at USI.
   - Gamma Phi Omega International Sorority, Inc.

The University of Southern Indiana is committed to the growth of these chapters and the addition of more NPHC and NALFO affiliated chapters in the future.
