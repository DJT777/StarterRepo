How to Join:

- NPHC/NALFO organizations have open recruitment periods determined by each specific organization.
- Requirements for joining vary by organization.
- Interested individuals should contact chapters directly through social media or email.
- Informational meetings will be held throughout the year, advertised on campus.
- Chapters may require transcripts and recommendations reflecting academic achievement and service.
