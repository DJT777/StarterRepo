Financial Transparency at the University of Southern Indiana (USI)

University Finance:

- Approximately 52.9% of the university's 2021-2022 current operating budget comes from state appropriations.
- Student fees make up 40.1% of the budget.
- Other sources contribute 7.0% of the budget.
- The state appropriation for fiscal year 2021-2022 is $62,823,461.
- The total investment in campus buildings and grounds as of June 30, 2021, was $465,584,592.

Financial Transparency Project:

The Financial Transparency Project was created by House Enrolled Act 1004-2011 through Indiana Code 5-14-3.6. It requires Indiana's publicly funded higher education institutions to submit audited financial statements, outstanding debt information, and other financial documents for public access and transparency.

Reports and Statements:

- Annual Financial Report: Provides audited financial statements for each year.
- Annual Operating Budget Report: Details the annual operating budget for each year.
- Federal Single Audit Report: Reports on the federal single audit for each year.
- Operating and Capital Budget Requests: Requests and presentations of operating and capital budgets for each year.
- Personnel Report 100-R: Provides personnel reports for each year.
- Purchasing Business Diversity Report: Reports on purchasing business diversity for each year.
- Space Report: Details the university's space usage for each year.
- USI Foundation Financial Statements: Provides financial statements for the USI Foundation for each year.
