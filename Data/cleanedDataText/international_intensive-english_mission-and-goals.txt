Intensive English Program at the University of Southern Indiana

Mission Statement:

The Intensive English Program (IEP) at the University of Southern Indiana aims to provide quality English language instruction and cultural, social, and academic orientation to non-native students. The program focuses on student-centered teaching practices and well-trained, culturally diverse faculty to prepare students for success in university academic programs or to achieve their professional and personal language goals. The IEP also supports students' integration into the target academic and general culture while respecting their own cultures, traditions, and values. As part of the Center for International Programs, the IEP enhances the international dimensions of the University and connects international students to the campus and local communities.

Student Goals:

- Prepare international students for academic studies at USI or for using English professionally or in social contexts.
- Provide ongoing support services to international students.
- Connect international students with other students at USI and the local community to immerse them in American culture.

Faculty Goals:

- Engage faculty members with high levels of professional conduct and teaching skills to teach IEP students.
- Support and engage faculty members in continuous professional development and program improvement.
- Stay informed and up to date with research supporting best teaching practices.

Long Term Program Goals:

- Maintain program accreditation with the Commission on English Language Program Accreditation (CEA).
- Build and promote the program's name and quality reputation through professional associations, activities, and recruitment efforts.
- Serve as an English language proficiency testing center for the TOEFL test.
- Develop special short-term language projects and bring groups from international professional institutions for English language skills development and Teacher Training.
- Enhance community outreach with local companies and institutions, attending local fairs, etc.
- Advocate for all international students.
- Engage with other member organizations like EnglishUSA and NAFSA for additional support and information updates.
