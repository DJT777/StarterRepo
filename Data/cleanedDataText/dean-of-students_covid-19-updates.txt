    Fall 2023 Covid Information:
        The COVID self-reporting form has been removed.
        Students who test positive for COVID should follow CDC guidelines.
        Students should contact faculty members for class and missed work arrangements.
        Complications due to COVID should be reported to the Dean of Students office.
        Faculty notices can be sent by the Dean of Students Office in emergency situations where students are unable to contact faculty members directly.
        Emergency situations include serious illness or hospitalization, serious accidents, or death in the family.
        Faculty notices are intended to be informational and should not replace direct communication between students and faculty members.
