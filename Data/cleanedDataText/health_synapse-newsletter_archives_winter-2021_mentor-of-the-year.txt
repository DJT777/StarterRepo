    Mentor of the Year:

        Jennifer Fehrenbacher, Assistant Professor of Dental Hygiene and Coordinator of the USI Dental Hygiene Clinic, received the Mentor of the Year award by Volunteer USI in August. She guides students through the Dental Hygiene Program, fostering clinical and leadership skills, cultural awareness, and civic engagement. She is dedicated to helping her students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Her nominators praise her teaching, advocacy for students, and commitment to providing a quality education.
