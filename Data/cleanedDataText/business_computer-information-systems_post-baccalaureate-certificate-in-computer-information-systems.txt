Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Computer Information Systems at Romain College of Business, University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Program Overview:

- Target audience: Those who have already earned a bachelor's degree in a different major.
- Employer recognition: Many employers consider the certificate equivalent to a bachelor's degree in CIS.
- Career transition: Provides skills to transition into a technology- or information systems-related field.
- Certification: Meets educational requirements to sit for the Certified Computer Professional Examination sponsored by the Institute for the Certification of Computer Professionals.
- Program requirements: 18 credit hours of courses, including four required courses and two electives.
- More information on the program and course requirements available.

Student Testimonial:

- Jamie Robey '11 M'14, Associate Resources Manager, shares her experience in the program.
- Highlights the importance of information systems in her field of Human Resources.

Gainful Employment Disclosure:

- Program completion time: Designed to be completed in 12 months.
- Total program cost: $5,801 for Indiana residents (additional costs for living expenses may apply).
- Debt upon program completion: N/A (fewer than 10 Title VI students completed the program within normal time).

Additional Information:

- Licensure requirements: No licensure requirements for this profession in all U.S. states and territories.
- More information on graduation rates, loan repayment rates, and post-enrollment earnings available.
