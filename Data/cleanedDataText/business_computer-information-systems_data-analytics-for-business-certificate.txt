        Program Description: The Data Analytics for Business Certificate offered by the Romain College of Business focuses on providing students with an in-depth understanding and hands-on experience in data analysis. The certificate covers topics such as data visualization and storytelling, advanced predictive analytics, and data management and preparation. The goal is to equip students with the necessary skills to work with data effectively, make sound decisions, and improve evidence-based decision-making abilities.

        Coursework: The certificate curriculum incorporates critical and cutting-edge components of data analytics. It includes best practices in data visualization, dashboard design, data exploration and storytelling, as well as the deployment and interpretation of automated machine learning tools. Students will also learn about data management and preparation for effective decision-making through business intelligence and analytics. Additionally, there are opportunities for students to pursue specific interests within the data analytics field, such as database and enterprise data concepts, research design, statistical methods, and more.
