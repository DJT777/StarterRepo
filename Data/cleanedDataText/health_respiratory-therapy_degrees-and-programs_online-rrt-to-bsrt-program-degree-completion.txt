RRT to BS in Respiratory Therapy Online Learning Program

The RRT to BS in Respiratory Therapy program offered by the University of Southern Indiana (USI) provides respiratory therapists with the opportunity to advance their careers and obtain a bachelor's degree entirely online. Here are the key details of the program:

Program Highlights:

- Complete in as few as 4 semesters
- 100% online program
- Cost: $345 per credit hour
- Currently accepting applications for the next class starting on 10/23/23
- Accredited by the Commission on Accreditation for Respiratory Care (CoARC)

Program Benefits:

- Advance in your career as a respiratory therapist
- Develop advanced skills in critical thinking, problem-solving, time management, and communication
- Gain knowledge and expertise to care for critically ill patients
- Prepare for advanced clinical respiratory therapist jobs and leadership positions
- Flexible online format allows you to continue working while studying

Admission Requirements:

- Completion of an associate degree in a CoARC-accredited respiratory therapy program
- Cumulative GPA of 2.3 on a 4.0 scale (individual review for GPA between 2.0 and 2.29)
- Possession of the Registered Respiratory Therapist (RRT) credential from the National Board for Respiratory Care (NBRC)
- Submission of the USI undergraduate application and official transcripts
- Non-native English speakers must provide proof of English proficiency (TOEFL or IELTS)

Program and Graduation Requirements:

- Transfer of credits earned in an accredited community college or university
- No limitation on the number of transferable credits
- A minimum of 30 credit hours must be earned from USI
- Core respiratory therapy courses (22-30 credits)
- Non-respiratory support courses (not more than 19 credits)
- Total program requirements: 120 credits
- Completion of REST courses with a minimum grade of "C"

Program Delivery and Technology Requirements:

- All courses offered online using a learning management system
- Access to a reliable computer with internet connection

USI's RRT to BS in Respiratory Therapy program offers working professionals the opportunity to further their education and enhance their respiratory therapy careers in a flexible and accessible online format.
