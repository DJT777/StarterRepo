Pre-Professional Student Resources

    Seminar in Medicine:

        Offered by Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education.
        A discussion-based course providing realistic information about the practice of medicine and the path to medical school.
        Covers topics such as MCAT preparation, applications, shadowing experiences, and personal statements.
        Recommended to enroll in HONS 291 in the Spring of sophomore year or approximately a year and a half before medical school application.
        Instructor approval required for enrollment.
