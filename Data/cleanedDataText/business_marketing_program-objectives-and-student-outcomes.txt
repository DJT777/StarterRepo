Marketing Program Objectives and Student Outcomes:

1. Program Objective: Building competency in fundamental marketing content
   - Measurement Tools: ETS - MFT (Business) - Marketing portion

2. Program Objective: Developing competency in the marketing planning process
   - Learning Outcomes: Developing a marketing plan including market segmentation, positioning, and marketing mix
   - Measurement Tools: Final project
   - Course Coverage: Marketing Management (MKTG 448)

3. Program Objective: Critical thinking
   - Learning Outcomes: Using relevant information/evidence, considering relevant viewpoints, identifying relevant assumptions, and developing clear logical conclusions
   - Measurement Tools: Class assignment
   - Course Where Assessed: Marketing Management (MKTG 448)

4. Program Objective: Ethical decision making
   - Learning Outcomes: Identifying ethical issues/problems, evaluating relevant stakeholders, evaluating implications of alternative courses of action, and developing logical conclusions
   - Measurement Tools: Class assignment
   - Course Where Assessed: Marketing Management (MKTG 448)
