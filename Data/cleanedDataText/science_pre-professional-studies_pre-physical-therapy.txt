Pre-Physical Therapy Program at the Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education, University of Southern Indiana (USI)

Overview:

USI's pre-physical therapy program provides comprehensive preparation for admission to top physical therapy graduate schools. The program focuses on field experience and hands-on instruction to ensure students are well-prepared for their future careers.

Program Details:

- Pre-PT Track: Students can enter the pre-PT track as freshmen and start taking pre-physical therapy courses immediately, allowing them to complete all physical therapy prerequisites within four years and 120 credit hours.
- Degree Options: Students can complete the pre-PT program while earning a bachelor's degree in either Biology or Exercise Science.
- Exceptional Preparation: USI offers outstanding preparation for students to compete for admission to physical therapy graduate programs nationwide.

Information and Resources:

- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ): Visit the program's FAQ page for answers to common questions about the path to Physical Therapy School and the application process.
- 4-Year Plans: Biology and Exercise Science majors can access 4-year plans to ensure they are on track to meet their academic and pre-PT requirements.
