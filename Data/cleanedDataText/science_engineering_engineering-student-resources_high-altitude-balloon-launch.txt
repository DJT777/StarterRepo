- High Altitude Balloon Launch:
    - Description of the project conducted by USI Engineering students in the ENGR 471 and ENGR 290 classes.
    - Use of helium-filled weather balloons carrying experimental devices, cameras, and GPS trackers.
    - Balloon ascends to 100,000 feet, bursts, and the pods descend with parachutes for recovery.
    - Students prepare the pods, plan flight operations, launch the balloon, track it, and recover the pods for analysis.
    - Information about past and upcoming High Altitude Balloon flights is available on the website.
