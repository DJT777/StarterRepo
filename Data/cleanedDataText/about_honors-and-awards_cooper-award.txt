H. Lee Cooper Core Curriculum Teaching Award

    Established in 2000
    Honors a USI faculty member who has made significant contributions to University Core Courses (UCC)
    Aims to further UCC goals
    Recipients receive a stipend funded by Ann and H. Lee Cooper III
