Program Description:

The finance program at the Romain College of Business prepares students for successful careers and graduate school opportunities. The program equips students with the skills needed to make sound financial decisions for businesses and households. Through coursework, students learn to analyze financial data, evaluate economic trends, and make informed financial decisions. The program covers diverse topics such as financial analysis, investments, risk, and valuation.

Career Opportunities:

Students who graduate with a degree in finance have various career options available to them. They can work in brokerage firms, commercial and investment banks, corporations, financial advisory and accounting firms, government agencies, insurance companies, real estate agencies, and more. Some of the potential careers include appraisers, loan officers, portfolio managers, financial analysts, real estate agents, financial planners, and stockbrokers.
