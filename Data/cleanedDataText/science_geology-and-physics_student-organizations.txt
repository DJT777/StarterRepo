Pott College of Science, Engineering, and Education - Student Organizations

Geology Club:
- Promotes camaraderie and scholarship through social activities and instructional field trips.
- Encourages the involvement of undergraduate and graduate students, faculty, and alumni.
- Faculty advisor: Dr. Joseph DiPietro.

Physics Club:
- Devoted to learning and practicing physics.
- Works on projects and takes educational trips to various Physics facilities in the United States.
- Engages in outreach activities such as the Science Fair, school visits, and research presentations at conferences.
- Faculty advisor: Dr. Kenny Purcell.
